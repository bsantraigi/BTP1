Acapulco, cuerpo y alma (English: Acapulco, Body and Soul), is a Mexican telenovela produced by Jos Alberto Castro in 1995 for Televisa. It is a remake of the Mexican telenovela produced by Televisa in 1985, T o nadie.
Patricia Manterola and Sal Lisazo star as the main protagonists, while Guillermo Garca Cant and Chantal Andere star as the main antagonists.
