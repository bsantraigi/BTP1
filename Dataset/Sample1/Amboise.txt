Amboise (pronounced [bwaz]) is a commune in the Indre-et-Loire department in central France. It lies on the banks of the Loire River, 27 kilometres (17 mi) east of Tours. Today a small market town, it was once home of the French royal court. The town of Amboise is also only about 18 kilometres (11 mi) away from the historic Chteau de Chenonceau, situated on the Cher River near the small village of Chenonceaux.
