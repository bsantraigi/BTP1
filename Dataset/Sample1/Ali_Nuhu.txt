Ali Nuhu Mohammed (born 15 March 1974) is a Nigerian film actor, producer, director, dancer and script writer. He acts in both Hausa and English movies and is often referred as the "King of Kannywood" by the media, Kannywood referring to the Hausa film industry headquartered in Kano, Nigeria.  Ali is most followed male celebrity in the Kannywood industry and Also the indigenous celebrity in Africa.
