The 201415 season was Olympiacos' 56th consecutive season in the Superleague Greece. Olympiacos also participated in the Greek cup.
Olympiacos won the domestic title for the fifth year in a row.
Olympiacos also participated in UEFA Europa League Knock-out Stage after being eliminated from the UEFA Champions League Group Stage.
On January 6 Olympiacos announced the end of the cooperation with head coach Mchel. Vtor Pereira was hired as the new manager of the team.
