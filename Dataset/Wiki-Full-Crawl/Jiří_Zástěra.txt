Ji Zstra (9 November 1913  15 August 1983) was a football player and manager. He made 198 appearances in the Czechoslovak First League, scoring three goals. He played three matches for Czechoslovakia. As a manager, Zstra led numerous teams. He led ATK Prague to an eighth-place finish in 1948. He later won the 195960 Czechoslovak First League with Spartak Hradec Krlov.
