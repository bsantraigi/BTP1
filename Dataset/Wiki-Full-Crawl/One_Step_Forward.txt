"One Step Forward" is a song written by Chris Hillman and Bill Wildes, and recorded by American country rock group The Desert Rose Band. It was released in October 1987 as the third single from the album The Desert Rose Band.  The song reached number 2 on the Billboard Hot Country Singles & Tracks chart, behind "Tennessee Flat Top Box" by Rosanne Cash. This song can also be heard on the video game Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas on the fictional radio station K-Rose.
