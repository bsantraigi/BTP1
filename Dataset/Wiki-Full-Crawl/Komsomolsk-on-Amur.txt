Komsomolsk-on-Amur (Russian: --, tr. Komsomolsk-na-Amure, IPA: [kmsmolsk nmur]) is a city in Khabarovsk Krai, Russia, located on the left bank of the Amur River in the Russian Far East. It is located on the Baikal-Amur Mainline, 356 kilometers (221 mi) northeast of Khabarovsk. Population: 263,906(2010 Census); 281,035(2002 Census); 315,325(1989 Census).
