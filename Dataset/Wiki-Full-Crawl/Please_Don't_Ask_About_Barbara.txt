"Please Don't Ask About Barbara" is a song written by Bill Buchanan and Jack Keller and performed by Bobby Vee.  It reached #15 on the Billboard Hot 100, #18 in Canada, and #29 in the UK in 1962.  It was featured on his 1962 album, A Bobby Vee Recording Session.The song was produced by Snuff Garrett.The single's B-side, "I Can't Say Goodbye", reached #90 in Canada and #92 on the Billboard chart.
