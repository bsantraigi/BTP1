Gmina Czuchw is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Czuchw County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. Its seat is the town of Czuchw, although the town is not part of the territory of the gmina.
The gmina covers an area of 361.65 square kilometres (139.6 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 10,180.
