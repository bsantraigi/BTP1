Amirizdwan Taj Bin Tajuddin (born 30 March 1986), commonly known as Taj is a Malaysian professional footballer who plays as a centre back and capped by Malaysian national team.
Born in Kota Bharu, Kelantan, Taj never seriously play football at his youth where he attended MRSM Gerik and later MRSM Kuala Terengganu. His football career began while completing his undergraduate studies at Universiti Teknologi MARA where he played for UiTM FC. During that time, Taj was commonly known by nickname Panjang which means tall in English, to describe his tall physic body compared to his other teammates. Taj was a late boomer which was not from regular state President Cup or youth teams.
