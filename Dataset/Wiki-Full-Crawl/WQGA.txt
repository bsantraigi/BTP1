WQGA (103.3 MHz, "103Q") is a commercial FM radio station licensed to Waycross, Georgia, and serving the Brunswick, Georgia, and Jacksonville, Florida, area.  The station is owned by iHeartMedia, Inc., through licensee AMFM Radio Licenses, L.L.C., and airs a Top 40 radio format.The station airs The Kidd Kraddick Morning Show, syndicated from Westwood One.
