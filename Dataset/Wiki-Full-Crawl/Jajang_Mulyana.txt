Jajang Mulyana (born 23 October 1988 in Jatinangor) is an Indonesian football player. He plays in Indonesia for Bhayangkara F.C.. He started his career with Persib Bandung then signed with Pelita Jaya. His natural position is striker, he is tall enough, strong & he has a good pace. Because of his good physichal, sometimes the coach of mitra kukar   used him as a defender in several match.
