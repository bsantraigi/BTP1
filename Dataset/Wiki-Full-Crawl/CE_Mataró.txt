Club Esportiu Matar is a Spanish football team based in Matar, in the autonomous community of Catalonia. Founded in 1939 it currently plays in Segona Catalana, holding home games at Municipal Carles Padrs, which has a capacity of 4,500.
The club competes in both association football and futsal, and also possessed a basketball team, whose women's section won three leagues from 1972 to 1974.
