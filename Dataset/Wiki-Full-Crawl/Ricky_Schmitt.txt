Ricky William Schmitt (born August 17, 1985) is a former gridiron football punter. He was signed by the Arizona Cardinals as an undrafted free agent in 2007. He played college football at Shepherd University.
Schmitt was also a member of the San Diego Chargers, Pittsburgh Steelers, Oakland Raiders, San Francisco 49ers, Tennessee Titans, Saskatchewan Roughriders, and BC Lions.
