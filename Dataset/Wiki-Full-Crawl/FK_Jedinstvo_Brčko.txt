Fudbalski klub Jedinstvo Brko (Serbian Cyrillic:   Jeco po) is a football club from the town of Brko, in the Brko District, Bosnia and Herzegovina. The club competed in the First League of the Republika Srpska, a second-tier competition in Bosnia and Herzegovina, but after being relegated it competes in the Second League of the Republika Srpska. After the football club FK Napredak Donji epak, as many times before, in the season 201617 in the Second League of the Republika Srpska (East) as the first place went, they had to cancel the entry into the higher league, due to the unsatisfied conditions for the First League of the Republika Srpska. And thus Jedinstvo Brko could take their place as second place and so promoted to the higher tier again.
The club used to compete in the Yugoslav Second League before the break-up of SFR Yugoslavia.
