The Wrong Object is a jazz-rock band from Belgium. The band was formed in 2002 by guitarist Michel Delville  and includes Marti Melia on saxophone and clarinet, Franois Lourtie on saxophones, Antoine Guenet on keyboards, Pierre Mottet on bass and electronics, and Laurent Delchambre on drums. Former members include Fred Delplancq on tenor sax, Damien Polard on bass, and Jean-Paul Estivenart (winner of the Django d'Or award for best young jazz musician in 2007) on trumpet.
