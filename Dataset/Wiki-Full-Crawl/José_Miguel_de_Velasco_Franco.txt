Jos Miguel de Velasco Franco (29 September 1795  13 October 1859) served as Vice President of Bolivia for one time (1829-1835) and President of Bolivia four times (1828, 1829, 1839-1841 and 1848) more times than anyone else other than Vctor Paz Estenssoro. During the third, and longest, of these temporary periods in office he approved a new constitution for Bolivia.
