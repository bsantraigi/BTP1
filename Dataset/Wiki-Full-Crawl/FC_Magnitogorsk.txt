FC Magnitogorsk (Russian:  ) is a Russian football team from Magnitogorsk. It played professionally from 1948 to 1949, 1959 to 2003 and in 2005. It plays in the Amateur Football League. It played on the second-highest level (Soviet First League and Russian First Division) in 19481949, 19581962, 19681969 and 19921993.
