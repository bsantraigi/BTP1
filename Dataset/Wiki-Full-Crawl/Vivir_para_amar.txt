Vivir para amar, is a Venezuelan telenovela produced by Antonio Franco for TVes. It is the first telenovela produced in the studios of TVes.Fedra Lpez and Jorge Reyes star as the main protagonists, Vanessa Mendoza  and Daniel Tern as co-protagonists.Written by Csar Cierra, directed by Jos Gregorio Scala and Jos Luis Limanski, and produced by Antonio Franco as executive producer and Vladimir Salazar as General producer.
