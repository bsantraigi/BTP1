Hilrio Rosrio da Conceio, OM (born 19 March 1939), known simply as Hilrio (Portuguese pronunciation: [ilaiu]), is a Portuguese former footballer who played as a left back.
He played his entire professional career with Sporting CP, appearing in nearly 450 official games and winning seven major trophies.An international for 12 years, Hilrio represented the Portugal national team at the 1966 World Cup.
