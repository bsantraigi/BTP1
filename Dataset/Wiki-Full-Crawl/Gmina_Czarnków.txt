Gmina Czarnkw is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Czarnkw-Trzcianka County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Czarnkw, although the town is not part of the territory of the gmina.
The gmina covers an area of 347.78 square kilometres (134.3 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 10,887.
