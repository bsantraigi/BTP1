En riktig jul ("A Real Christmas") was the 2007 "SVT Christmas calendar", broadcast by Sveriges Television (SVT).The TV series was produced in JanuaryMarch 2007. It was recorded at lstensgatan ("lsten Street") in lsten, Stockholm Municipality and at rsta torg in rsta, southern Stockholm. It was also recorded in studio until June.It was directed by Simon Kaijser da Silva and written by Pernilla Oljelund and Hans Rosenfeldt.
