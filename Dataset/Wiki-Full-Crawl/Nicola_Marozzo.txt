Nicola Marozzo (born 1 March 1965) is a former Italian racing driver from Zumpano, near Cosenza. Gaining initial experience in Italian Formula Three from 1984 to 1986, he competed in 3 CART Indy Car World Series races for Euromotorsport in 1991 and 1992. He attempted two more races in 1992 but failed to start in one and failed to qualify for another.  He failed to finish any of his 3 races and scored no championship points. He also participated in the 1988 24 Hours of Le Mans.
He won different races in Italy, on two and four wheels (with bike and car).
