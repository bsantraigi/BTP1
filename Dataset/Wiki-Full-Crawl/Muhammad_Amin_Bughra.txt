Muhammad Amin Bughra also Muammad Amn Bughra (19011965) (Uyghur:   ) (  ),   , Chinese: ; pinyin: MhnmdYmn (sometimes known by his Turkish name Mehmet Emin Bugra) was a Turkic Muslim leader, who planned to set up an independent state, the First East Turkestan Republic.
