Jernimo Mndez Arancibia (American Spanish: [xeonimo mendes aansibja]; September 25, 1887 14 June 1959) was a Chilean politician who served as provisional president.
He was born in Chaaral, where he completed his secondary studies. In 1914 he graduated as an MD from the Universidad de Chile. President Pedro Aguirre Cerda appointed him Minister of the Interior. At the death of President Aguirre Cerda on November 25, 1941, he became provisional President. He called for presidential elections that were won by Juan Antonio Ros on February 1, 1942.
