Iolanda Cristina Gigliotti (Italian: [jolanda kristina ditti]; 17 January 1933  3 May 1987), professionally known as Dalida (Egyptian Arabic: ), was a French vocalist and actress, born in Egypt to Italian parents. She won the Miss Egypt beauty contest in 1954 and began a 30-year singing career in 1956, selling 140 million albums and singles worldwide. She committed suicide in 1987.
