Maya Mayuram (English: Illusive Peacock) is a 1993 Indian Malayalam romantic drama film directed by Sibi Malayil and written by Ranjith. It stars Mohanlal, Shobana, Revathi, Thilakan, Sukumari, Kaviyoor Ponnamma and Shanthi Krishna in pivotal roles. Its music was composed by Reghu Kumar while Johnson provided the background score. The film was produced by R. Mohan under the banner Grahalakshmi Films.
