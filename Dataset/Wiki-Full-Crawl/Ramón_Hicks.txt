Ramn Angel Mara Hicks Cceres (born 30 May 1959 in Asuncin) is a Paraguayan former football winger. A well travelled player at club level he turned out for teams in his homeland (Club Libertad), Uruguay (Nacional), Spain (CE Sabadell FC, Real Oviedo and Elche CF), Argentina (Independiente) and Bolivia (Club San Jos). He appeared as an international for Paraguay and was in the squad for the 1986 FIFA World Cup. Altogether he made 37 appearances for the national team, scoring six goals.
