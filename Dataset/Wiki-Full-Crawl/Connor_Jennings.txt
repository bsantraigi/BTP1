Connor Joseph Jennings (born 29 October 1991) is an English professional footballer who plays as a Midfielder or striker for League Two club Tranmere Rovers.
Jennings started his career with Stalybridge Celtic in the 200809 season, staying with them until halfway through 201112, where he scored over 50 goals in over 100 games, before moving on to Scunthorpe United. He played once for the England national C team, scoring one goal.
