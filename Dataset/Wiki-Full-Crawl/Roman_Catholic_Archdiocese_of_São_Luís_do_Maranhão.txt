The Roman Catholic Archdiocese of So Lus do Maranho (Latin: Archidioecesis Sancti Ludovici in Maragnano) is a Latin archdiocese in Brazil.
Its cathedral is a World Heritage Site (Minor): Catedral Metropolitana Nossa Senhora da Vitria Catedral Metropolitana Nossa Senhora da Vitria.  It is located in the city of So Lus do Maranho.
