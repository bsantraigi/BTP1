Garry Mendes Rodrigues (born 27 November 1990) is a Dutch-born Cape Verdean footballer who plays as a winger for Galatasaray.
He formerly played for PAOK, Elche CF, XerxesDZB, FC Boshuizen, ADO Den Haag, Dordrecht and Bulgarian side Levski Sofia.Born in the Netherlands, in February 2013, Rodrigues announced that he would like to play for Cape Verde, and made his debut in August 2013. He represented the nation at the 2015 Africa Cup of Nations.
He is a cousin of Jerson Cabral.
