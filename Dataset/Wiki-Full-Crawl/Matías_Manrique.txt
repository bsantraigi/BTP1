Matas Eduardo Manrique (born 1 November 1980 in Mendoza) is an Argentine football defender currently playing for ublense in Chile.
Manrique started his professional playing career in 2001 with Independiente. He played for Arsenal de Sarand between 2002 and 2004, when he returned to Independiente.
In 2006 Manrique joined Uruguayan team Danubio and in 2007 he joined Pearol where he was part of the squad that won the Clausura 2008 tournament.
