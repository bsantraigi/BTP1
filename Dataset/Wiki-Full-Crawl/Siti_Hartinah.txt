Raden Ayu Siti Hartinah (23 August 1923  28 April 1996) was the wife of the second Indonesian president, Suharto. She is usually known as Ibu Tien to Indonesians, but is also known as Siti Hartinah Soeharto (the difference in spelling is due to Dutch Colonial era spelling).
Madame Suharto was also widely acknowledged to be a close confidant and political advisor to Suharto.
