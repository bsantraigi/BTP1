Djurgrdens IF Fotbollsfrening, commonly known as Djurgrdens IF, Djurgrden Fotboll (official name), Djurgrden [j(r)on], and (especially locally) Djurgr'n [j(r)], Dif or DIF, is the association football department of Djurgrdens IF and is based at Tele2 Arena in the Johanneshov district of Stockholm.
Fans of the club, called djurgrdare, are found all over Stockholm and Sweden. However, the stermalm district of Stockholm City Centre, where Djurgrden's former home ground Stadion is situated, is considered the club's heartland.
Formed in 1891 and playing in the first Swedish tier, Allsvenskan, the club has won the league eleven times and the cup five times. The league titles have been won during three eras. The first period was the 1910s, when they won four league titles. The second era occurred in the 1950s and 60s, when Djurgrden also won the league four times. The most recent era was the first half of the 00s when they won both the league and the cup three times.
Djurgrden is affiliated to the Stockholms Fotbollfrbund.
