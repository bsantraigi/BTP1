Kara Saun is a German-born fashion and costume designer and CEO of Kara Saun LLC, an LA-based fashion and costume design house. She was the costume designer on The Disney Channels franchise Descendants and Descendants 2.
Saun launched a teen-driven lifestyle brand "The Fashion FairyGodmother".
Saun appeared on Season One of the series, Project Runway. She was the costume designer on MTVs America's Best Dance Crew (1-5), NBCs The Sing-Off (1-3). She also costume designed Disneys Make and "Jabbawockeez MUS.I.C. Vegas Show.
