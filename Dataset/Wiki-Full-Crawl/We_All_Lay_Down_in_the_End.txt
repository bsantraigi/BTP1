We All Lay Down In The End (Chinese: ) is Taiwanese Mandopop artist Stanley Huang's (Chinese: ) 6th Mandarin studio album. It was released on 5 December 2008 by Warner Music Taiwan ().Like previous albums, one more edition was released, We All Lay Down In The End (AERO Version)( (AERO)), on 10 January 2009, including a bonus DVD containing three behind-the-scene footages with music videos.
