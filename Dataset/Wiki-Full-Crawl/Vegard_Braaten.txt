Vegard Btnes Braaten (born 30 June 1987 in Troms) is a Norwegian professional footballer playing for PostNord-ligaen club Alta.
He grew up near Srkjosen, with his twin brother and fellow footballer Thomas, and made his senior debut for Nordreisa at the age of fourteen. He joined Tippeligaen side Troms in 2004. He only got three league games for the club, played mainly on the reserve team, and was loaned out to Tromsdalen and Alta. In December 2008 both Thomas and Vegard Braaten were signed by Alta on a permanent basis.
In June 2010 he joined Lokeren. In December 2011 he joined Bod/Glimt from 2012, together with his twin brother Thomas Braaten.
