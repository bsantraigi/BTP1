Csar Martn Villar (born 3 April 1977), known simply as Csar, is a Spanish retired footballer who played as a central defender.
Over the course of 13 seasons he appeared in 200 La Liga games, mostly in representation of Oviedo and Deportivo, after which he had a six-month spell in England with Bolton.
A Spanish international during five years, Csar appeared in Euro 2004.
