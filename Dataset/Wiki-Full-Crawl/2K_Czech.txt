2K Czech, s.r.o. (formerly Illusion Softworks, a.s.) was a Czech video game developer based in Brno. Founded as Illusion Softworks, by Petr Vochozka in 1997, the company was acquired by Take-Two Interactive in January 2008 and subsequently organized under the 2K Games label, becoming 2K Czech. The company was merged into Hangar 13 in 2017.
