lex Dario Aguinaga Garzn (Spanish pronunciation: [aleks ainaa]; born July 9, 1968 in Ibarra) is an Ecuadorian retired football midfielder and manager of Categora Primera A team Itag. As a player, he is one of the all-time cap leaders for Ecuador with 109 matches, scoring 23 goals. Aguinaga is known as one of the best Ecuadorian footballers of all time. On the club level, Aguinaga has played for Deportivo Quito, Necaxa, Cruz Azul and LDU Quito.
