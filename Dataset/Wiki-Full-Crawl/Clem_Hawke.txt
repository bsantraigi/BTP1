Arthur Clarence "Clem" Hawke (5 March 1898  23 December 1989) was the General Secretary of the Australian Labor Party in South Australia 191920, and a Congregationalist minister.
He was the father of Bob Hawke, Prime Minister of Australia 198391; and brother of Bert Hawke, MHA for Burra Burra, South Australia 192427 and Premier of Western Australia 195359.
