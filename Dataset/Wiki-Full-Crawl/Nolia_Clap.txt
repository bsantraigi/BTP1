"Nolia Clap" is a single by UTP (composed of Juvenile, Wacko and Skip), produced by Donald XL Robertson and recorded in Los Angeles, California, from the album The Beginning of the End.
The music video was filmed in the Magnolia Projects in New Orleans, Louisiana, and has  cameos by B.G. (rapper) and Nelly. The remix on the CD single featured Bun B, Earl Hayes (died 2014), Slim Thug, T.I., and Z-Ro, rapping over Donald XL Robertson's track also used on Mos Def's "Dollar Day (Surprise, Surprise)".
