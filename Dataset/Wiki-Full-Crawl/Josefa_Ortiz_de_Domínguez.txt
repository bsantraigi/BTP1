Mara Josefa Crescencia Ortz TllezGirn, popularly known as Doa Josefa Ortiz de Domnguez or La Corregidora (April 19, 1773    March 2, 1829) was an insurgent and supporter of the Mexican War of Independence, which fought for independence against Spain, in the early 19th century. She was married to Miguel Domnguez, corregidor of the city of Quertaro, hence her nickname.
