Commune Tuowice is a rural commune (administrative district) in Opole County, Opole Voivodeship, in south-western Poland. Its seat is the village of Tuowice, which lies approximately 22 kilometres (14 mi) south-west of the regional capital Opole.
The commune covers an area of 81.13 square kilometres (31.3 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 5,504.
