Playalitical (born Dustin Robbins March 07 1982 in Denver,CO) is an American rap artist and producer and the owner of Illuminated Entertainment Group. Known for his deep raspy voice, traditional lyricism, and word play he discusses lived stories. He's done songs with Snoop Dogg, E40, Young Joc, The Game, Lil Flip and Chino XL as well as produced albums for Bizzy Bone, Young Droop, Lighter Shade of Brown and Zig Zag of NB Ridaz.
