The Tsugaru Line (, Tsugaru-sen) is a railway line operated by East Japan Railway Company (JR East). It connects Aomori Station and Minmaya Station on the Tsugaru Peninsula in western Aomori Prefecture.
The section of the line between Aomori Station and Naka-Oguni Station is a part of the Tsugaru-Kaiky Line connecting Honshu and Hokkaido.
