Hold Your Fire is the second studio album of the hard rock band FireHouse. It was released in June 1992. The album spent thirty weeks in the Billboard 200 Top Albums chart peaking at # 23.The album spawned several hit singles like "Reach for the Sky", "Sleeping With You" and another signature love ballad in the form of "When I Look Into Your Eyes". The album was certified Gold by the RIAA in the United States.
