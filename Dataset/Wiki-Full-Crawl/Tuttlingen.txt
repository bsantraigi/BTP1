Tuttlingen is a town in Baden-Wrttemberg, capital of the district Tuttlingen.  Nendingen, Mhringen and Elingen are three former municipalities that belong to Tuttlingen. The district (Kreis) includes several surrounding towns including Trossingen, Spaichingen, and Mhlheim an der Donau.
Tuttlingen is located in Swabia east of the Black Forest region in the Swabian Alps.
