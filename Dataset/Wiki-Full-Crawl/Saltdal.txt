Saltdal (Northern Sami: Slt) is a municipality in Nordland county, Norway. It is part of the traditional district of Salten. The administrative centre of the municipality is the village of Rognan. Other villages in Saltdal include Rkland and Lnsdal.
The 2,216-square-kilometre (856 sq mi) municipality is the 23rd largest by area out of the 422 municipalities in Norway.  Saltdal is the 212th most populous municipality in Norway with a population of 4,702.  The municipality's population density is 2.3 inhabitants per square kilometre (6.0/sq mi) and its population has increased by 0.3% over the last decade.
