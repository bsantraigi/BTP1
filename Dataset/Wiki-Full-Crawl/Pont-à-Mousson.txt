Pont--Mousson (French pronunciation: [p.ta.mu.s]) is a commune in the Meurthe-et-Moselle department in northeastern France.
Population (1999): 14,592 (Mussipontains). It is an industrial town (mainly steel industry), situated on the Moselle River. Pont--Mousson has several historical monuments, including the 18th century Premonstratensian abbey.
