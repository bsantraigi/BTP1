Gmina Wiele is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Czarnkw-Trzcianka County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Wiele, which lies approximately 27 kilometres (17 mi) west of Czarnkw and 75 km (47 mi) north-west of the regional capital Pozna.
The gmina covers an area of 428.32 square kilometres (165.4 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 12,572 (out of which the population of Wiele amounts to 5,940, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 6,632).
