Stanford-le-Hope is a town and Church of England parish situated in the county of Essex, England. Often known locally simply as Stanford,  the town is within the unitary authority of Thurrock and located 23.8 miles (38.4 km) east of Charing Cross in London. Its principal claim to fame is that Joseph Conrad lived and wrote there. Unlike some other areas of Thurrock, Stanford-le-Hope is surrounded by countryside and farmland.
