Lobuya (Russian: ; Yakut: ) is a rural locality (a selo), and one of two settlements in the Town of Srednekolymsk of Srednekolymsky District in the Sakha Republic, Russia, in addition to Srednekolymsk, the administrative center of the district. It is located 18 kilometers (11 mi) from Srednekolymsk. Its population as of the 2010 Census was 3, of whom all three were male, down from 8 recorded during the 2002 Census.
