Rebellion was an annual professional wrestling pay-per-view (PPV) event held by the World Wrestling Federation (called World Wrestling Entertainment in 2002) and broadcast exclusively in the United Kingdom. Rebellion was also shown on the Viewers Choice service in Canada, but was never televised in the United States. Rebellion had its own theme song titled "Rebellion".
The 2001 event features The Alliance period for the WWF. All matches has one member of the WWF taking on one member of The Alliance. Over the course of the evening, the WWF won six matches, which the Alliance won three.
In 2002, it was a SmackDown! brand exclusive show. This event would be final Rebellion event after all UK-exclusive pay-per-views ceased in 2003.
