The Cheetah Girls 2 is the soundtrack album to the 2006 Disney Channel Original Movie, The Cheetah Girls 2. The album was released on August 15, 2006, through Walt Disney Records. The album was produced by 
Kenny Ortega, Debra Martin Chase, Raven-Symon and Whitney Houston. The album features appearances from Drew Seeley and Belinda.
The songs on the soundtrack are a diverse blend of pop, R&B, Latin, and dance-pop. The album debuted at number five on Billboard 200 album chart. On November 14, 2006, the album was certified Platinum in the United States selling over one-million copies.
