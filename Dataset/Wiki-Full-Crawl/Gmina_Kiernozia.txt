Gmina Kiernozia is a rural gmina (administrative district) in owicz County, d Voivodeship, in central Poland. Its seat is the village of Kiernozia, which lies approximately 20 kilometres (12 mi) north of owicz and 61 km (38 mi) north-east of the regional capital d.
The gmina covers an area of 76.03 square kilometres (29.4 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 3,638.
