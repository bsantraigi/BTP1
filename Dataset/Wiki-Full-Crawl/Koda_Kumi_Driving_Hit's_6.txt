Koda Kumi Driving Hit's 6 is the eighth remix album by Japanese singer-songwriter Kumi Koda. It was released on March 19, 2014 and came in at #19 on Oricon, only remaining on the charts for four weeks. As with her two previous Driving Hit's, the remixes were performed in drum and bass, dubstep and house. 
It became her first Driving Hit's album to be released in a CD+DVD version (Beach Mix came in CD+DVD), with the DVD housing the documentary of her Taiwan Live 2013.
