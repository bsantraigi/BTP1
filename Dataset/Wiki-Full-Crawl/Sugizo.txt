Yne Sugihara ( , Sugihara Yne), born Yasuhiro Sugihara ( , Sugihara Yasuhiro, on July 8, 1969 in Hadano, Kanagawa, Japan), better known exclusively by his stage name Sugizo, is a Japanese musician, singer, songwriter, composer, record producer, actor, writer and activist. He is best known as lead guitarist and violinist of the rock band Luna Sea.
He started his solo career in 1997 and has since collaborated with many artists. In 2007 he became a member of the trance/world music act Juno Reactor, and of the Japanese rock supergroup S.K.I.N., and in 2009 officially joined the heavy metal band X Japan. He is also part of the psychedelic jam band Shag and the shamanic electronica unit S.T.K (Sensual Technology Kooks).In addition to being known to experiment with many musical genres, mostly rock, psychedelic and electronica, he is known for his political ideas and views, being an anti-war, anti-nuclear and environmental activist.
