enski nogometni klub Krka (English: Women's Football Club Krka), commonly referred to as NK Krka or simply Krka, was a Slovenian women's football club from Novo Mesto. Krka won the Slovenian Women's League eight times, and played several seasons in UEFA competitions. The best result in those was playing the Round of 32 in the 201011 UEFA Women's Champions League season. The club was dissolved in 2016.
