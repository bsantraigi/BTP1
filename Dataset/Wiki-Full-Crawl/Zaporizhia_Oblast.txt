Zaporizhia Oblast (Ukrainian:  , translit. Zaporiz'ka oblast; Russian:  ); also referred to as Zaporizhzhya (Ukrainian: ), is an oblast (province) of southern Ukraine. Its capital is Zaporizhia. The oblast covers an area of 27,183 km, and its population was 1,739,500(2017 est.).
This oblast is an important part of Ukraine's industry and agriculture.
