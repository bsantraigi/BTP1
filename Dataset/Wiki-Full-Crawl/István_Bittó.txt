Count Istvn Bitt de Srosfa et Ndasd (Srosfa, Kingdom of Hungary (today Blatn na Ostrove, Slovakia), 3 May 1822  Budapest, 7 March 1903) was a Hungarian politician who served as Speaker of the House of Representatives of Hungary from 10 September 1872 to 23 March 1874 and as Prime Minister of Hungary from 1874 to 1875.
