Felix Edward Hbert (October 12, 1901  December 29, 1979), known as F. Edward Hbert, was an American congressman from Louisiana. He represented the New Orleans-based 1st congressional district as a Democrat for 18 consecutive terms, from 1941 until his retirement in 1977. He remains Louisiana's longest-serving U.S. representative.
