Madden NFL 12 is an American football video game based on the National Football League published by EA Sports and developed by EA Tiburon. It was released on August 30, 2011 in North America and Asia, September 1, 2011 in Australia, and September 2, 2011 in Europe. It was available on August 27, 2011 to EA Sports Season Ticket subscribers for a three-day trial. It was the final Madden NFL game to be released on the PlayStation 2 and PlayStation Portable.
The demo was released on August 9, featuring a game between the Green Bay Packers and Chicago Bears.
