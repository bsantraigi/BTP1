Nogometni klub Zagorje (English: Zagorje Football Club), commonly referred to as NK Zagorje or simply Zagorje, is a Slovenian football club which plays in the town of Zagorje ob Savi. They play in the Slovenian Third League. The club was founded in 1923. The club have a long-standing local rivalry with NK Rudar Trbovlje, which is known as Zasavje derby.
