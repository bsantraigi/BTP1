Villiers is a former municipality in the district of Val-de-Ruz in the canton of Neuchtel in Switzerland.
The municipalities of Boudevilliers, Cernier, Chzard-Saint-Martin, Coffrane, Dombresson, Engollon, Fenin-Vilars-Saules, Fontainemelon, Fontaines, Les Geneveys-sur-Coffrane, Les Hauts-Geneveys, Montmollin, Le Pquier, Savagnier and Villiers merged on 1 January 2013 into the new municipality of Val-de-Ruz.
