Miguel Filipe Machado de Albuquerque is a Portuguese politician of the PSD and the current President of the Regional Government of Madeira. He took office as leader of the Social Democratic Party of Madeira on 10 January 2015. He was a former mayor (presidente da Cmara in Portuguese) of Funchal, Madeira. 
Albuquerque likes gardening, growing many types of roses in his Duchess of Braganza Rose Garden.
