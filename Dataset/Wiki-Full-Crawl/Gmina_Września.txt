Gmina Wrzenia is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Wrzenia County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Wrzenia, which lies approximately 46 kilometres (29 mi) east of the regional capital Pozna.
The gmina covers an area of 221.84 square kilometres (85.7 sq mi), and as of 2012 its total population is 45,523 (out of which the population of Wrzenia amounts to 29,564, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 15,959).
