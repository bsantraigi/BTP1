Senda de gloria (English title: Track from glory) is a Mexican telenovela produced by Ernesto Alonso for Televisa in 1987. The telenovela recreates historical events in Mexico between 1916 and 1939. It starred by Eduardo Yez, Julieta Rosen, Ignacio Lpez Tarso, Blanca Snchez, Roxana Chvez and Anabel Ferreira.
