Gmina Raniw is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Kolbuszowa County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. Its seat is the village of Raniw, which lies approximately 16 kilometres (10 mi) east of Kolbuszowa and 26 km (16 mi) north of the regional capital Rzeszw.
The gmina covers an area of 96.77 square kilometres (37.4 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 7,185.
