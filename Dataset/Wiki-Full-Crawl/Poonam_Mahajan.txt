Poonam Mahajan alias Poonam Vajendla Rao (born 9 December 1980) is a
Member of Parliament from Mumbai North Central and a politician of the  Bharatiya Janata Party , or  BJP. She was appointed as President of Bhartiya Janta Party Yuva Morcha BJYM in December 2016, Youth Wing of BJP. She was also the President of the Basketball Federation of India, a rank which she is the first female to hold.
