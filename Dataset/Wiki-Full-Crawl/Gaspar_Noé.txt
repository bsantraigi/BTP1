Gaspar No (Spanish: [gaspa noe]; French: [as.pa n.e]; born December 27, 1963) is an Argentine filmmaker living in France. He is the son of Argentine painter, writer and intellectual Luis Felipe No. He is mostly known for his five feature films: I Stand Alone (1998), Irrversible (2002), Enter the Void (2009), Love (2015), and Climax (2018).
His film Love premiered at the Grand Theatre Lumiere in the Midnight Screenings section at the 2015 Cannes Film Festival.
