Dejan Peri (born 22 September 1970) is a retired Serbian handballer and current coach. He played as a goalkeeper for RK Dinamo Panevo, RK Crvena Zvezda, Pelister Bitola, Atltico Madrid BM,  SD Teucro, Celje Pivovarna Lako, FC Barcelona, MVM Veszprm and is a former member of the Serbia national handball team. Currently he is in charge of the goalkeepers of RK Vardar.
