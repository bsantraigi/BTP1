The Vicente Caldern Stadium (Spanish: Estadio Vicente Caldern [estajo iente kaldeon]) was the home stadium of Atltico Madrid since its completion in 1966 to 2017, with a current seating capacity of 54,907 and located on the banks of the Manzanares, in the Arganzuela district of Madrid, Spain. The stadium was originally called the Estadio Manzanares, but this was later changed to the Vicente Caldern Stadium, in honour of their long-term President Vicente Caldern.
