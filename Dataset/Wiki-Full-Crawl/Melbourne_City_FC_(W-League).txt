Melbourne City FC, also known as the Melbourne City Women or simply as City, represents Melbourne City in the W-League, the top division women's football (soccer) league in Australia.  Founded in 2015, the club has its training and administration based at the City Football Academy in Melbourne and plays home games at both the CB Smith Reserve in the northern suburb of Fawkner and at AAMI Park near the Melbourne city centre. The current manager of the team is Rado Vidoi.
