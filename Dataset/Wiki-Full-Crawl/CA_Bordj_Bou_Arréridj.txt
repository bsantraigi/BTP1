Chabab Ahly Bordj Bou Arrridj (Arabic:    ), known as CA Bord Bou Arrridj or simply CABBA for short, is an Algerian football club based in Bordj Bou Arreridj, founded in 1931. The club colours are yellow and black. Their home stadium, Stade 20 Aot 1955, has a capacity of 15,000 spectators. The club is currently playing in the Algerian Ligue Professionnelle 1.
