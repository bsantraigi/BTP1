Michael "Mike" Glita (born July 7, 1982) is an American musician, producer, songwriter, and artist manager. He is the singer/songwriter for the band Knights In Paris. Glita was also the bassist and background vocalist (screamer) for New Jersey Post-hardcore band Senses Fail, the producer and drummer for Love Automatic, and the drummer for Tokyo Rose.Glita grew up in West Orange, NJ, where he graduated from Seton Hall Preparatory School in 2000 and then attended Rutgers University in New Brunswick, New Jersey. During his time at Rutgers, he was also a member of New Jersey rock band Tokyo Rose. Before his career as a musician, Glita attended Stratton Mountain School in Stratton, Vermont for most of high school as a ski racer. After spending 4 years in the music department at William Morris Endeavor and as a manager at Vector Management, which is part of Live Nation Entertainment, he now has his own entertainment company, The Glita Group.
