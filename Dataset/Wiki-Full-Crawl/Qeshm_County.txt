Qeshm County (Persian:  ) is a county in Hormozgan Province in Iran. The capital of the county is Qeshm.  At the 2006 census, its population was 103,881, in 22,642 families. As of 2010 it has a population of 113 846.[1] The county is divided into three districts: the Central District, Shahab District, and Hormuz District. The county has four cities: Hormuz, Qeshm, Dargahan, and Suza
