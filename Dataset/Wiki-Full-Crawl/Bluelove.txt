Bluelove is the second Korean mini album of South Korean rock band CNBLUE. It was released on May 19, 2010 by FNC Entertainment and distributed by Mnet Media. Before the album was released, "Love Light" (Korean:  ) was released as the first digital single on May 10, 2010. After it was released "Love" was promoted as the lead single. The album contain five new tracks plus previously released English track, "Let's Go Crazy" from their debut Japanese EP, Now or Never.
The album was released in Taiwan in June 2010 by Warner Music Taiwan in two versions:  import limited edition and commemorate edition.
