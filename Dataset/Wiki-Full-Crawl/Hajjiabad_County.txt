Hajjiabad County (Persian:  ) is a county in Hormozgn Province in Iran. The capital of the county is Hajjiabad, the capital is located about 100 km north of Bandar Abbas (the central city of Hormozgan Province) and is best known for its citrus produce.  At the 2006 census, the county's population was 62,442, in 15,056 families. The county is divided into three districts: the Central District, Fareghan District, and Ahmadi District. The county has two cities: Fareghan and Hajjiabad.
