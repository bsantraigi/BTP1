Hlne Christine Marie Rigoine de Fougerolles (French pronunciation: [eln d ful]; born 25 February 1973) is a French actress. She is the daughter of Alain Rigoine de Fougerolles and Anne Saumay de Laval. Initially planning to become a beautician, she began to study acting at the age of 15. She later attended acting classes in Paris and briefly at the Actors Studio in New York City.
She made her first ever stage appearance in the title role of Occupe Toi d'Amlie at the Thtre de la Michodire in Paris from September 2012 until May 2013.
She was formerly married to ric Hubert in 1997 and divorced in 2004. They have a daughter named Shana, born in 2003.
