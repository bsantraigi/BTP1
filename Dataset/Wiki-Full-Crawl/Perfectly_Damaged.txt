Perfectly Damaged is the sixth studio album from Swedish singer Mns Zelmerlw. It was released on 3 June 2015 through Warner Music Sweden. On 11 May 2015, Zelmerlw revealed the artwork and track list for the album. The album was recorded in Stockholm, Copenhagen and London in 2015 during spring and summer. The album was produced by Winterlude, Anton Malmberg Hrd af Segerstad, Joy Deb, Fredrik Sonefors and Martin Bjelke.
Perfectly Re:Damaged, a re-issue of the album, was released on 13 May 2016.
