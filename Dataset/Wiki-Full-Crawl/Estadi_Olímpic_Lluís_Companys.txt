Estadi Olmpic Llus Companys (Catalan pronunciation: [stai ulimpi uis kumpas], formerly known as the Estadi Olmpic de Montjuc and Estadio de Montjuic) is a stadium in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain. Originally built in 1927 for the 1929 International Exposition in the city (and Barcelona's bid for the 1936 Summer Olympics, which were awarded to Berlin), it was renovated in 1989 to be the main stadium for the 1992 Summer Olympics.The stadium has a capacity of 55,926 (67,007 during the 1992 Olympics), and is located in the Anella Olmpica, in Montjuc, a large hill to the southwest of the city which overlooks the harbor.
