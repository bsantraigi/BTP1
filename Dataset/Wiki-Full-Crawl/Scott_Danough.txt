Scott Danough is a co-founder and guitar player for Bleeding Through. Scott played with Bleeding Through from its foundation in 1999 and appeared on the albums Dust to Ashes, Portrait of the Goddess, This is Love, This is Murderous, and The Truth. In 2013, Scott rejoined Bleeding Through for their farewell tour and left Falling To Pieces. Currently Scott is working on a new project with current and former members of Race Traitor, Walls Of Jericho, 3 Inches Of Blood, and Catharsis.
