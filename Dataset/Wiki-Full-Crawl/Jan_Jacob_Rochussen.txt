Jan Jacob Rochussen (Dutch pronunciation: [jn jakp roxsn]; 23 October 1797  21 January 1871) was a Dutch politician. He served as Finance Minister from 1840 to 1843 and Governor-General of the Dutch East Indies from 1845 to 1851. He served as Chairman of the Council of Ministers from 18 March 1858 to 23 February 1860.
