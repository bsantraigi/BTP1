In Full Effect is the third album by old school hip hop-electro funk group Mantronix, and the first Mantronix album released on Capitol Records. In Full Effect was the highest charting hip-hop album for Mantronix, reaching #18 on the 1988 Billboard Magazine Top R&B Albums chart. In Full Effect was also rapper MC Tee's final Mantronix album. According to the liner notes, the album was the first-ever digital recording to be mastered from DAT rather than reel-to-reel tape.
