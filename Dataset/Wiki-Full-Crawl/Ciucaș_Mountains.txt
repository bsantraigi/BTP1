The Ciuca Mountains (in Romanian, Munii Ciuca) is a mountain range in Romania.
The highest peak is Vrful Ciuca (Ciuca Peak), at 1,954 meters.  The headwaters of the Buzu River, the Teleajen River, the Trlung River and many others are located there.
In Romania, the Ciuca Mountains are considered part of the Curvature Carpathians.  Geologically, according to the divisions of the Carpathians, the range is part of the Moldavian-Muntenian Carpathians, of the larger group of the Outer Eastern Carpathians.
The Cheia village is close to the mountain.
