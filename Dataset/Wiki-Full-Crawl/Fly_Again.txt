Fly Again is the third album from Kristine W. The album was packaged as a double disc album. The album peaked at #12 on the Top Electronic Albums chart.  The first cd was the full-length album, while the second album contained remixes from Fly Again, continuously mixed by Chris Cox, formerly of Thunderpuss.
"Fly Again", "Save My Soul", "The Wonder of It All", and "I'll Be Your Light" were released as singles.
