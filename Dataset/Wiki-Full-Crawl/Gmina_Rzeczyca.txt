Gmina Rzeczyca is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Tomaszw Mazowiecki County, d Voivodeship, in central Poland. Its seat is the village of Rzeczyca, which lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) north-east of Tomaszw Mazowiecki and 61 km (38 mi) east of the regional capital d.
The gmina covers an area of 108.29 square kilometres (41.8 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 4,971.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Spaa Landscape Park.
