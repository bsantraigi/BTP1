Los nicos (Spanish: "The uniques") is a 2011 Argentine superhero live-action television series aired by El Trece in the prime time. The "uniques" are a superhero team working as a covert operation unit. Main actors are Arnaldo Andr, Mariano Martnez and Griselda Siciliani, the two main supervillains are played by Favio Posca and Carlos Belloso.
