Gregory Michael Bryk (born 19 August 1972) is a Canadian film and television actor. He has had a number of film roles, and may be best known as a series regular in three different television showsReGenesis, XIII: The Series and Bitten. He is also well known for his role in 2018 game Far Cry 5, for which he provided his voice and likeness for Joseph Seed, the lead antagonist. He will also reprise his role as Joseph Seed in the upcoming sequel Far Cry: New Dawn.
