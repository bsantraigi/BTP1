Blackfoot Gypsies are an American four-piece rock band from Nashville, Tennessee, United States. Their music is influenced by blues, punk rock, southern rock, soul, Americana and country. They are known for their DIY aesthetic and extensive touring.
In 2013, the band headlined Muddy Roots Music Festival. As of July 3, 2014, the band has reached nearly 4,000 Facebook likes.
On January 23, 2015, the band signed with label Plowboy Records. On January 28, the band released their new single, "Under My Skin", and announced April 14, 2015, as the release date of their second album, Handle It. The record will be their first on Plowboy Records.
