The New Fellas was the title of The Cribs' second album released in 2005. It placed at No. 11 in the NME's 'Albums Of The Year' in 2005. The song "Hey Scenesters!" was placed 42nd in a poll for the top 50 Indie anthems of all time, which was published in the influential music magazine NME in May 2007, one of the only contemporary bands included in the list. 
In December 2009, Q Magazine made it one of their "Albums of the Century" in their year end issue.
Ben Duff (The Mardous) sings backing vocals on Martell.
