The Neam or Ozana is a right tributary of the river Moldova in Romania. It starts at the confluence of headwaters Rul Neamului cel Mare and Rul Neamului cel Mic. It discharges into the Moldova near Timieti. It flows through the villages Boboieti, Pipirig, Pligeni, Stnca, Leghin, Lunca, Vntori-Neam, Dumbrava, Timieti and the town Trgu Neam. Its basin area is 419 km2 (162 sq mi).
