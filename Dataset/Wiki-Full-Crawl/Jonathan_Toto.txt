Jonathan Toto (born 30 March 1990) is a French footballer of Cameroonian heritage, who is a striker that last played for Greek side Doxa Drama in 2013.
Toto began his senior career in Cameroon with Canon Yaound before moving to Italy to sign for Legnano. After they were wound up, Toto moved to Scotland to sign as an amateur for Greenock Morton before being quickly released. He moved to Singapore with toile in 2011 and then to under-23 side Courts Young Lions.
