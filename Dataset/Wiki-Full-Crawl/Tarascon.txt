Tarascon (Occitan: Tarascon), sometimes referred to as Tarascon-sur-Rhne, is a commune situated at the extreme west of the Bouches-du-Rhne department of France in the Provence-Alpes-Cte d'Azur region. Inhabitants are referred to as Tarasconnais or Tarasconnaises. The patron saint of the city is Martha of Bethany, whose motto is "Concordia Felix".
