Ferdinand Dauk (also known as Fernando Daucik; 30 May 191014 November 1986) was a Slovak football player and manager. Dauk was the manager of several La Liga clubs, most notably Barcelona, Atltico Bilbao, Atltico Madrid and Real Zaragoza. During his career, he managed La Liga clubs in 488 matches, won three La Liga titles and won the Copa del Generalsimo on five occasions and won three La Liga/Copa doubles. He died in Alcal de Henares.
