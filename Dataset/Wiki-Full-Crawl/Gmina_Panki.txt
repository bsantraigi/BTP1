Gmina Panki is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Kobuck County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Panki, which lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) west of Kobuck and 73 km (45 mi) north of the regional capital Katowice.
The gmina covers an area of 55.03 square kilometres (21.2 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population was 4,997.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Upper Liswarta Forests Landscape Park.
