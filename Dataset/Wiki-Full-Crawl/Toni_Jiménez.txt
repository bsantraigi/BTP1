Antonio Jimnez Sistachs (Spanish pronunciation: [antonjo ximene]; born 12 October 1970), sometimes known just as Toni, is a Spanish retired footballer who played as a goalkeeper, and is the goalkeeping coach for English club Tottenham Hotspur.
He appeared in 236 La Liga matches over nine seasons, seven of those spent with Espanyol. He also represented in the competition Rayo Vallecano and Atltico Madrid.
Toni helped Spain win the gold medal at the 1992 Summer Olympics.
