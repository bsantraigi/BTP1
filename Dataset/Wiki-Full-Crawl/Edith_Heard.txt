Edith Heard (born 1965)  is a British researcher in epigenetics, working in Paris, France. She is a Professor at the Collge de France, holding the Chair of Epigenetics and Cellular Memory, and since 2010 has been Director of the Genetics and Developmental Biology department at the Curie Institute (Paris), France. From January 2019, she will be Director General of the European Molecular Biology Laboratory (EMBL). Heard is noted for her studies of X chromosome inactivation.
