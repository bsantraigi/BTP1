Gmina Bargw Kocielny is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Augustw County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. Its seat is the village of Bargw Kocielny, which lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) south-west of Augustw and 76 km (47 mi) north of the regional capital Biaystok.
The gmina covers an area of 187.57 square kilometres (72.4 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 5,783.
