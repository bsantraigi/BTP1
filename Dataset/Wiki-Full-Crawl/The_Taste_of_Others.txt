The Taste of Others (French: Le Got des autres [l u dezot]) is a 2000 French film. It was directed by Agns Jaoui, and written by her and Jean-Pierre Bacri. It stars Jean-Pierre Bacri, Anne Alvaro, Alain Chabat, Agns Jaoui, Grard Lanvin and Christiane Millet.
It won the Csar Award for Best Film, Best Supporting Actor, Best Supporting Actress and Best Writing in 2001, and was nominated for an Academy Award for Best Foreign Language Film.
