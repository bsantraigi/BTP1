Stade Gnral Seyni Kountch (SGSK), is a multi-purpose stadium in Niamey, Niger.   Used for football matches, it is home to the Niger national squad, as well as Niger Premier League clubs Sahel SC, Olympic FC de Niamey, Zumunta AC and JS du Tnr, as well as club competitions such as the Niger Cup. The venue is also sometimes used for rugby union.
