Wendy Anne-Marie Stapleton (born 8 July 1954) is an Australian pop/rock singer-songwriter, and musical theatre and television actress. Stapleton has performed as a backing singer, session musician and a solo artist; she fronted various Melbourne-based groups including Wendy Stapleton Band (19761978) and Wendy & the Rocketts [sic] (19801985) which had a top 30 hit single with "Play the Game" in June 1983.
