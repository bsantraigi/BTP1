Tijara(Hindi: ) is a city and a municipality in Alwar district of the Indian state of Rajasthan. Tijara comes under the NCR area and situated 48 km to the northeast of Alwar. The nearest railway station to Tijara is Khairthal.
Bhiwadi is a census town in Tijara Tehsil. Also Tijara is the biggest industrial area of Rajasthan and part of the historical Ahirwal region. Tijara is dominated by Yadav, Meo community.
