Mujhse Dosti Karoge! (translation: "Will you be my friend?") is a 2002 Indian Hindi romantic drama film directed by Kunal Kohli and produced by Aditya Chopra and Yash Chopra under the banner of Yash Raj Films. The film follows the love triangle of three friends played by Uday Chopra, Hrithik Roshan, Rani Mukerji, and Kareena Kapoor.
