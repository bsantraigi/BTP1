The Conservative Democrats of Slovakia (Slovak: Konzervatvni demokrati Slovenska, KDS) is a defunct Slovak political party established by four MPs (Frantiek Mikloko, Vladimr Palko, Rudolf Bauer and Pavol Minrik) who belonged to the Christian Democratic Movement, but left it on 21 February 2008 over disagreements with the party leader. The party was established in July 2008. The party was dissolved in 2014.KDS announced that Frantiek Mikloko would contest the 2009 presidential election. Mikloko received 5.41% of the vote.
