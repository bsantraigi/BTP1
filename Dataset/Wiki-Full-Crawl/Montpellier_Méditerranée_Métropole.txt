Montpellier Mditerrane Mtropole is the mtropole, an intercommunal structure, centred on the city of Montpellier. It is located in the Hrault department, in the Occitanie region, southern France. It was created in January 2015, replacing the previous Communaut d'agglomration de Montpellier. Its population was 457,760 in 2014, of which 279,845 in Montpellier proper.
