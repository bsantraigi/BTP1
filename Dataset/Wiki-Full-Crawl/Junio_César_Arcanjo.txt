Jnio Csar Arcanjo (born 11 January 1983), commonly known as Juninho, is a footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Anpolis. He has also played for Atltico Mineiro and, on loan, for Fluminense and Bahia.
On 25 February 2011, Juninho signed for South Korean club Daegu FC on a one-year loan. Juninho made his Daegu FC debut on 5 March  against Gwangju FC at Guus Hiddink Stadium in a 23 loss.
