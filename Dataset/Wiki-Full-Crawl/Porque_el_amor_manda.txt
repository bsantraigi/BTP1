Porque el amor manda (literal translation Because Love Rules, but simply known in English speaking marketings as Love Rules) is a 2012 Mexican psychological comedy thriller telenovela produced by Juan Osorio for Televisa. It is a remake of the Colombian telenovela El secretario, produced by Juan Andrs Flrez in 2011.Fernando Colunga and Blanca Soto star as the protagonists, while Claudia lvarez and Erick Elas star as the antagonists.
