Thadayam (English: Clue) is a 1997 Tamil action film directed by Ramesh Balakrishnan. The film features Vijayashanti, Ramki and Indraja in lead roles. The film, produced by Jothi Prasad Srinivasan, had musical score by Deva and was released on 28 November 1997. The film is partly inspired by the Telugu film Gang Leader.
