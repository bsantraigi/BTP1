Jon Bruning (born April 30, 1969) is an American politician who served as the 31st Attorney General of Nebraska from 2003 to 2015. A member of the Republican Party, he previously represented the 3rd district in the Nebraska Legislature from 1997 until 2003. He was a candidate in the United States Senate election in Nebraska, 2012, losing the Republican nomination to Deb Fischer and in the Nebraska gubernatorial election, 2014, also losing the nomination to Pete Ricketts.
