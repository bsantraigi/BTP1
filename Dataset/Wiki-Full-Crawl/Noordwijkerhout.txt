Noordwijkerhout ([nortikrut] (listen)) is a town and municipality in the western Netherlands, in the province of South Holland. The municipality covers an area of 23.42 km2 (9.04 sq mi) of which 0.81 km2 (0.31 sq mi) is water and had a population of 16,491 in 2017.  The town is in the bulb growing region (the Duin- en Bollenstreek) of the Netherlands, famed for its tulips.
