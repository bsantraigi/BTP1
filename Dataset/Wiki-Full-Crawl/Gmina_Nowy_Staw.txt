Gmina Nowy Staw is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Malbork County, Pomeranian Voivodeship, in northern Poland. Its seat is the town of Nowy Staw, which lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) north of Malbork and 36 km (22 mi) south-east of the regional capital Gdask.
The gmina covers an area of 114.38 square kilometres (44.2 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 7,840 (the population of Nowy Staw amounts to 4,447, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 3,393).
