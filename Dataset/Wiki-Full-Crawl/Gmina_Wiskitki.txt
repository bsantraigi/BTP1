Gmina Wiskitki is a rural gmina (administrative district) in yrardw County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Wiskitki, which lies approximately 5 kilometres (3 mi) north-west of yrardw and 44 km (27 mi) west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of 150.94 square kilometres (58.3 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 9,298.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Bolimw Landscape Park.
