Universiti Brunei Darussalam (UBD; translation University of Brunei Darussalam; Jawi:   ) is the first university in Brunei. It was established in 1985 and has since become the largest university in the country in terms of student enrollment and curriculum offered.UBD is ranked 100th in Asia by the 2019 QS Asia University Rankings
, and 323rd in the world by the 2019 QS World University Rankings.
