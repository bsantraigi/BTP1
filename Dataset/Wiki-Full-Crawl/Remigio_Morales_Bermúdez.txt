Remigio Morales Bermdez (September 30, 1836  April 1, 1894) served as the 37th President of Peru from 1890 to 1894. His predecessor until 1890 was Andrs Avelino Cceres, his successor was Justiniano Borgoo in 1894. He died while still in office.
His future grandson, who he would never live to see, Francisco Morales Bermdez, became president of Peru from 1975 to 1980.
