Gmina Rowienica is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Jarosaw County, Subcarpathian Voivodeship, in south-eastern Poland. Its seat is the village of Rowienica, which lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-west of Jarosaw and 43 km (27 mi) east of the regional capital Rzeszw.
The gmina covers an area of 70.69 square kilometres (27.3 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 6,395 (6,215 in 2013).
