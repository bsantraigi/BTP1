The San Diego Gulls are a professional ice hockey team in the American Hockey League (AHL) which began playing in the 201516 season. Based in San Diego, California and affiliated with the National Hockey League's Anaheim Ducks, the Gulls play their home games at the Pechanga Arena San Diego. The team is the fifth hockey team in San Diego to use the Gulls name.  The Gulls are a relocation of the former Norfolk Admirals (AHL) franchise, joining six other AHL franchises to form a new AHL Pacific Division.
