The Tony Danza Tapdance Extravaganza was an American mathcore band, from Murfreesboro, Tennessee. The band was founded (sans a vocalist) in Monroe, Louisiana by the members were Layne Meylain, Mason Crooks, Brad Thompson, and Mike Butler.  After these members joined together, they relocated to Murfreesboro, TN to find a frontman, whom became Jessie Freeland. The group derived their name (as a joke) from celebrity Tony Danza, upon one of his professions being tapdancing. The Tony Danza Tapdance Extravaganza released four studio albums before disbanding in 2012.
