Las Gonzlez is a Venezuelan telenovela written by Cesar Miguel Rondn and Mnica Montaez, and produced by Venevisin in 2002. The series lasted for 91 episodes and was distributed internationally by Venevisin International.On June 22, 2002, Venevisin started broadcasting Las Gonzlez weekdays at 9:00 pm with the series airing its final episode on 27 November, 2002.Gaby Espino and Jorge Reyes starred as the main protagonists, accompanied by Adrin Delgado, Alba Roversi and Vctor Cmara.
