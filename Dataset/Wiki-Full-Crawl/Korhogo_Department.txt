Korhogo Department is a department of Poro Region in Savanes District, Ivory Coast. In 2014, its population was 536,851 and its seat is the settlement of Korhogo. The sub-prefectures of the department are Dassoungboho, Kanoroba, Karakoro, Kimou, Kombolokoura, Komborodougou, Koni, Korhogo, Lataha, Nafoun, Napioldougou, N'Ganon, Niofoin, Sirasso, Sohouo, and Tioroniaradougou.
