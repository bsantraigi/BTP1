The Ibusuki Makurazaki Line (, Ibusuki Makurazaki-sen) is a railway line in Kagoshima Prefecture, Japan, operated by the Kyushu Railway Company (JR Kyushu). It connects Kagoshima-Ch Station in Kagoshima, to Makurazaki Station in Makurazaki, paralleling the eastern and southern coasts of Satsuma Peninsula. Excluding the Okinawa Monorail in Okinawa, this is the southernmost JR railway line in Japan, with Nishi-yama Station being the southernmost station overall.
