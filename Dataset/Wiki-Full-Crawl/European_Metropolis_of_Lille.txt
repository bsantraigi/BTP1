The Mtropole Europenne de Lille (MEL) - "European Metropolis of Lille" is the mtropole, an intercommunal structure, centred on the city of Lille. It is located in the Nord department, in the Hauts-de-France region, northern France  bordering both the Flemish and Walloon regions of Belgium. It was created in January 2015, replacing the previous Communaut urbaine de Lille, and covers that part of the Lille metropolitan area that lies in France. Its population was 1,154,103 in 2014, of which 238,381 in Lille proper.
