Philippe Ramos (born 1966) is a French film director, screenwriter and film editor. He directed an adaptation of Moby-Dick: Capitaine Achab with Valrie Crunchant and Frdric Bonpart in 2004.
Ramos is considered to be associated with the "new" French New Wave, alongside directors such as Yves Caumon, Jean-Paul Civeyrac, and Franois Ozon. He directed the 2011 historical film The Silence of Joan.
