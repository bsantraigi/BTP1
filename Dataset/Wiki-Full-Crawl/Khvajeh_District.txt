Khajeh District (Persian:  ) is a district (bakhsh) in Heris County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 30,151, in 7,001 families.  The District has one city: Khajeh. The District has three rural districts (dehestan): Bedevostan-e Gharbi Rural District, Mavazekhan-e Sharqi Rural District, and Mavazekhan-e Shomali Rural District.
