KALZ (96.7 FM, "Power Talk 96.7") is a commercial radio station located in Fowler, California, broadcasting to the Fresno, California, area.  KALZ airs a talk radio format. Initially, the station broadcast as "Alice 102.7" on 102.7 MHz from 1998 to early 2006. Originally, the station was known as "Smooth Jazz 96.7" (KEZL); that format was used on KJZN (105.5 FM).  Under ownership of iHeartMedia, Inc., its studios are located on Shaw Avenue in North Fresno, and the transmitter tower is in the unincorporated Fresno County community of Calwa, southeast of Fresno.
KALZ broadcasts two channels in HD.Alice 96.7 changed to Power Talk 96.7 on January 1, 2013.
