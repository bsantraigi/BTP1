The Municipality of Sveti Andra v Slovenskih Goricah (pronounced [seti anda  slenski itsax]; Slovene: Obina Sveti Andra v Slovenskih goricah) is a small municipality in northeastern Slovenia. It lies in the Slovene Hills. Until 1995 it was part of the Municipality of Ptuj and was then included in the Municipality of DestrnikTrnovska Vas. It became an independent municipality in 1998. The administrative centre of the municipality is the village of Vitomarci. Traditionally the area was part of the Styria region. The municipality is now included in the Drava Statistical Region.
