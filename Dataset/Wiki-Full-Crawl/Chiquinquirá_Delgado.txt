Mara Chiquinquir Delgado Daz (born August 17, 1972 in Maracaibo, Venezuela) is a Venezuelan TV host, model, and actress. She was a co-host of Mira Quien Baila on the Univision network in the United States. Prior to that, she was the host of Despierta Amrica!, the morning show on Univision, from 2010 until 2012.
