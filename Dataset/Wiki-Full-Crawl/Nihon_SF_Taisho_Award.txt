The Nihon SF Taish Award (SF, Nihon Esu Efu Taish, lit. "Japan SF Grand Prize") is a Japanese science fiction award. It has been compared to the Nebula Award as it is given by the Science Fiction and Fantasy Writers of Japan or SFWJ. The Grand Prize is selected from not only Science Fiction novels, but also various SF movies, animations, and manga.
Special Awards (, Tokubetsush) are awarded to the works that is considered to be special by the juries.  Since 2011, Special Services Award (, Tokubetsu krsh) or Award of Merit (, Ksekish) is presented to the deceased person.
