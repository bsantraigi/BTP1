FK Kpetdag Agabat (Russian: ) is a Turkmen football club based in Ashgabat, currently playing in the okary Liga. Their home stadium is Kpetdag Stadium which can hold 26,000 people. Kpetdag traditional kit colours are blue and white. Having won 6 Turkmen championships and 6 Cup of Turkmenistan, they are one of the country's most successful clubs.
