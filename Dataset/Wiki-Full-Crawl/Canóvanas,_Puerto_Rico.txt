Canvanas (Spanish pronunciation: [kanoanas]) is a municipality in Puerto Rico (U.S.), located in the northeastern region, north of Juncos and Las Piedras; south of Loza; east of Carolina; and west of Ro Grande. Canvanas is spread over 7 wards and Canvanas Pueblo (the downtown area and administrative center). It is part of the San Juan-Caguas-Guaynabo Metropolitan Statistical Area.
