Jab Tak Hai Jaan is the soundtrack album, composed by A. R. Rahman, to the 2012 Hindi film Jab Tak Hai Jaan, directed by Yash Chopra that stars Shahrukh Khan, Katrina Kaif and Anushka Sharma. The film was produced by Aditya Chopra under his own banner Yash Raj Films. The album has nine tracks and was released on 9 October 2012 on the firm's official record label YRF Music.
