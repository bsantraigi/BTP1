Kahak District (Persian:  ; formerly Nofel Loshato District) is a district (bakhsh) in Qom County, Qom Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 14,621, in 4,194 families.  The District has one city: Kahak. The district was named after Neauphle-le-Chteau, where Ayatollah Khomeini spent his exile in France. The district has two rural districts: Fordu Rural District and Kahak Rural District.
