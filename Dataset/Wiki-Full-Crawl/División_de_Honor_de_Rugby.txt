The Divisin de Honor is the top club competition for rugby union in Spain. Championships are organised by the Federacin Espaola de Rugby (Spanish Rugby Federation). The tournament was first held in 1953 and consists of 12 teams for the 2017-18 season. Clubs are relegated to and promoted from Divisin de Honor B. The winner each year qualifies for the European Challenge Cup, the second-tier European rugby competition.
The current Divisin de Honor champions are VRAC Quesos Entrepinares from Valladolid, in Castile and Len.
