Bc Trung B (literally North Central Region, and often translated as North Central Coast) is one of the regions of Vietnam. It consists of six provinces: Thanh Ha, Ngh An, H Tnh, Qung Bnh, Qung Tr, Tha ThinHu. The last two provinces were the northernmost provinces of South Vietnam until 1975.
North Central Coast (Bc Trung B) - 6 provinces: Thanh Ha, Ngh An, H Tnh, Qung Bnh, Qung Tr and Tha ThinHu. In the Nguyn dynasty, this area (except Tha Thin) was known as Hu Trc K (the area located in the left of Tha Thin).
