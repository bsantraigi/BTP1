Per Anders Otto Bill (born 13 February 1958) is a Swedish politician of the Moderate Party who has been Governor of Gvleborg County since 1 August 2015. He was a member of the Riksdag from 1994 to 2015.  Eisenhower Fellowships selected Per Bill in 1999 to represent Sweden. He is brother-in-law of Karin Enstrm, the former Minister for Defence.
