Jorge Fabin Vargas Gonzlez (Spanish pronunciation: [xoxe fajam baaz onsales]; born 8 February 1967) is a Chilean politician. He served as councillor of Pichilemu between September 1992 and December 1996, and as the 38th Mayor of Pichilemu, between December 1996 and November 1998, and from November 1999 to July 2007.
In November 2017 he was elected a member of the regional board of O'Higgins, representing the Cardenal Caro Province.
