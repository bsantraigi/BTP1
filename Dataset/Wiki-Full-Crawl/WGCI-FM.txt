WGCI-FM (107.5 FM) is an Urban Contemporary radio station that has a city of license of Chicago, Illinois under ownership of iHeartMedia, Inc. (formerly known as Clear Channel Communications until September 2014).
WGCI broadcasts with 3700 watts (3.7 kilowatts) at 107.5 megahertz (MHz) from atop the Willis Tower in Downtown Chicago, and has studios located in the Illinois Center complex on Michigan Avenue.  Like many Clear Channel-owned urban radio stations, it uses the slogan "Chicagos #1 For Hip Hop and R&B".
