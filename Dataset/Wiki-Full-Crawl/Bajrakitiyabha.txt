Princess Bajrakitiyabha (Thai: ; Thai pronunciation: [pt..r().k.t.j.p]; RTGS: Phatchara Kitiyapha; affectionally known as Princess Pa or Patty, born 7 December 1978) is a Thai royal diplomat and princess of Thailand, the first grandchild of King Bhumibol and Queen Sirikit of Thailand, and the only one of the seven children of King Maha Vajralongkorn born to his first wife Princess Soamsavali.
