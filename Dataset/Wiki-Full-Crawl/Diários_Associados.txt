The Dirios Associados, or Associated Dailies, are a union of Brazilian communication media created by Assis Chateaubriand.
Dirios Associados owned Rede Tupi, the first Brazilian Television Network, through its affiate, the Rede de Emissoras Associadas, from 1950 to 1980.
Today the group has 50 vehicles of communication, which are: 
15 Newspapers 
12 Radio networks
8 Television networks
9 Internet portals and 5 other sites
1 Foundation and 5 other companies
