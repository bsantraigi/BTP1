Wenshan Zhuang and Miao Autonomous Prefecture (simplified Chinese: ; traditional Chinese: ; pinyin: Wnshn Zhungz Mioz Zzhzhu)  is an autonomous prefecture in southeastern Yunnan province, People's Republic of China, and the easternmost prefecture-level division of the province. It borders Baise, Guangxi to the east, Vietnam's H Giang Province to the south for 438 kilometres (272 mi), Honghe Hani and Yi Autonomous Prefecture to the west, and Qujing to the north.
