Pushkinsky District (Russian:  ) is an administrative and municipal district (raion), one of the thirty-six in Moscow Oblast, Russia. It is located in the northern central part of the oblast. The area of the district is 571.47 square kilometers (220.65 sq mi). Its administrative center is the city of Pushkino. Population: 177,510 (2010 Census);  163,439(2002 Census); 123,630(1989 Census). The population of Pushkino accounts for 58.0% of the district's total population.
