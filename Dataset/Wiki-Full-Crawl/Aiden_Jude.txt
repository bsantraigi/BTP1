Aiden Jude is a 15 year old songwriter, a producer and DJ based in New York City.He has had releases on some of the world's leading dance music labels including Lowly Palace, Crowd Records, Armada Music, CR2 Records, Ensis Records, and Tronic Stomp.He is the founder of Crowd Records, Risky Business Presents, an event promoter and entertainment group.
He has been featured on media outlets such as Dancing Astronaut, DJ Mag, Vice 'Thump', Daily Beat, Hype Magazine, Beatport News, EDM Sauce, Your EDM, Music Times, and many more.Aiden Jude's music is charting and featured on radio stations and DJ sets around the world.
