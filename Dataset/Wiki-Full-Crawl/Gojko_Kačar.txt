Gojko Kaar (Serbian Cyrillic:  , pronounced [jko ktar]; born 26 January 1987) is a Serbian footballer, who plays as a midfielder for Anorthosis Famagusta. 
He made senior international debut in 2007. A Serbian international on 40 occasions he represented 2007 UEFA European Under-21 Championship , where won a silver medal, 2008 Summer Olympics and 2010 FIFA World Cup.
