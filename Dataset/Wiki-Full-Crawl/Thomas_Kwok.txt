Thomas Kwok Ping-kwong (Chinese: ; pinyin: Gu Bngjing; Cantonese Yale: Gwok Bnggng; born October 6, 1951) is a Hong Kong billionaire, and the former joint chairman and managing director (with his brother Raymond Kwok) of Sun Hung Kai Properties, the largest property developer in Hong Kong. He received a five-years prison sentence for bribery in 2014.
