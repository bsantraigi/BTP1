The Long Island Rough Riders is an American soccer team based in South Huntington, New York, United States. Founded in 1994, the team plays in USL League Two, the fourth tier of the American Soccer Pyramid.
The team plays its home games at Hofstra University Soccer Stadium. The team's colors are white and blue.
The team has a sister organization, the Long Island Rough Riders Women, who play in United Women's Soccer.
