Gmina Komnice is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Czstochowa County, Silesian Voivodeship, in southern Poland. Its seat is the village of Komnice, which lies approximately 22 kilometres (14 mi) north-east of Czstochowa and 79 km (49 mi) north of the regional capital Katowice.
The gmina covers an area of 147.85 square kilometres (57.1 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 13,821.
