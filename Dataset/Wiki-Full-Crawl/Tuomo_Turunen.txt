Tuomo Turunen (born 30 August 1987) is a Finnish footballer. He currently represents Kotkan Tyven Palloilijat, playing in Ykknen the Finnish second division. He plays as a defender or midfielder. Before moving to Trelleborg, he played for FC Honka, FC KooTeePee and IFK Gteborg. He is also a member of Finland national under-21 football team. In 2008, he was selected as the best under-21 player in Finland.
In December 2008, Honka and Helsingborg IF were negotiating about selling Turunen to the Swedish club, but Turunen stayed in Espoo. In July 2009, he was sold to IFK Gteborg and signed for 3.5 years.
