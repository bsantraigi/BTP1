Lankaran (Lenkoran, Lnkran) is a rayon (administrative district of Azerbaijan). It surrounds, but does not include, the city of Lankaran (which is technically a separate administrative region). Lankaran district was established as an administrative district on August 8, 1930. The territory of the district is 1,539 km2, with 66,700 hectares covering dry areas. There are 2 towns, 8 settlements and 83 villages in the Lankaran district. The distance from Baku to the regional center is 268 km.
