Gmina Zbszynek is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in wiebodzin County, Lubusz Voivodeship, in western Poland. Its seat is the town of Zbszynek, which lies approximately 20 kilometres (12 mi) east of wiebodzin, 41 km (25 mi) north-east of Zielona Gra, and 66 km (41 mi) south-east of Gorzw Wielkopolski.
The gmina covers an area of 94.42 square kilometres (36.5 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 8,376 (out of which the population of Zbszynek amounts to 5,087, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 3,289).
