Gmina Sabnie is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Sokow County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Sabnie, which lies approximately 13 kilometres (8 mi) north of Sokow Podlaski and 95 km (59 mi) east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of 107.92 square kilometres (41.7 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 3,941 (3,870 in 2013).
