Ghosts Can't Do It is a 1989 American romantic crime fantasy comedy film written and directed by John Derek, and starring Bo Derek and Anthony Quinn.The film was shot in 1989 and released on home video the same year in Australia. In the United States, the film received a theatrical release in 1990. It received heavily negative reviews from critics, and won the 1990 Golden Raspberry Award for Worst Picture, tying with The Adventures of Ford Fairlane.
In a cameo, it features future American President Donald Trump, receiving a Raspberry Award for Worst Supporting Actor.
