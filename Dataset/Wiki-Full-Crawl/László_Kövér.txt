Lszl Kvr (Hungarian: [laslo kver]; born 29 December 1959) is a Hungarian politician and the current Speaker of the National Assembly of Hungary. He was the acting President of Hungary from 2 April 2012 to 10 May 2012, after the resignation of Pl Schmitt.
He is a founding member of Fidesz from 1988, and he served as Minister without portfolio for the Civilian Intelligence Services during the first Viktor Orbn administration. In 2000 he was appointed leader of the party, but he resigned from his position in the next year.
