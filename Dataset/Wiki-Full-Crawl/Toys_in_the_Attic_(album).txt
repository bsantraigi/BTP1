Toys in the Attic is the third studio album by American rock band Aerosmith, released April 8, 1975 by Columbia Records. Its first single release, "Sweet Emotion," was released a month later on May 19 and "Walk This Way" was released on August 28 in the same year. The album is the band's most commercially successful studio LP in the United States, with eight million copies sold, according to the RIAA.The album was ranked No. 229 on Rolling Stone's list of The 500 Greatest Albums of All Time. The album's title track and RunD.M.C.'s version of "Walk This Way" are part of The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame's 500 Songs that Shaped Rock and Roll list.
