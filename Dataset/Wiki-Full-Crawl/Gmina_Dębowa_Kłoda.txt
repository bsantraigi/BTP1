Gmina Dbowa Koda is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Parczew County, Lublin Voivodeship, in eastern Poland. Its seat is the village of Dbowa Koda, which lies approximately 12 kilometres (7 mi) east of Parczew and 50 km (31 mi) north-east of the regional capital Lublin.
The gmina covers an area of 188.29 square kilometres (72.7 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 3,991 (3,968 in 2014).
