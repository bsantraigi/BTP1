The city of Disraeli is located in the Municipalit rgionale de comt des Appalaches in Quebec, Canada. It is part of the Chaudire-Appalaches region and the population is 2,570 as of 2009. It was named after British statesman and writer Benjamin Disraeli. 
The city of Disraeli forms an enclave in the territory of the parish of Disraeli and the two are separate legal entities.
