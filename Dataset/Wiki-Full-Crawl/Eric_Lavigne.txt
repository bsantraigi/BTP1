Eric T. Lavigne (born November 4, 1972 in  Victoriaville, Quebec) is a retired professional ice hockey defenceman who played one game in the NHL with the Los Angeles Kings during the 199495 NHL season. Lavigne was selected in the 2nd round (25th overall) in the 1991 NHL Entry Draft by the Washington Capitals.
Lavigne also played in the IHL, AHL, and later in the LNAH.
