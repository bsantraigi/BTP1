Gmina Dugosiodo is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Wyszkw County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Dugosiodo, which lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) north-east of Wyszkw and 73 km (45 mi) north-east of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of 167.45 square kilometres (64.7 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 7,649 (7,922 in 2013).
