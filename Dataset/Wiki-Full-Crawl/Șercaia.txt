ercaia (German: Schirkanyen; Hungarian: Srkny; Turkish: Saruhan) is a commune in Braov County, Romania. It is composed of three villages: Hlmeag (Halmagen; Halmgy; Halma), ercaia and Vad (Waadt, Waden; Vd).
At the 2011 census, 84% of inhabitants were Romanians, 10.6% Hungarians, 3.7% Roma and 0.8% Germans.
