TVN24 BiS (formerly TVN24 Biznes i wiat) is a Polish 24-hour hybrid business and world news channel, sister station to TVN24. It was launched on 1 January 2014, replacing TVN CNBC. TVN24 BiS belongs to the TVN Group which in turn is controlled by the Scripps Networks Interactive. As the world's first hybrid business and International news channel, most of the programmes are a combination of both format, although there're some exceptions.
The channel is available on all digital platforms in Poland (Cyfrowy Polsat and NC+), as well as most cable networks, satellite for viewers in the EU, Europe and around the world. The channel only competitor is Polsat News 2, sister network of Polsat News owned by Polsat which is also a hybrid business news and world news channel, replaced Polsat Biznes.
