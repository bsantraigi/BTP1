The Republic of Buryatia (Russian:  , tr. Respublika Buryatiya, IPA: [rspublk brtj]; Buryat:  , translit. Buryaad Ulas, pronounced [brt ls], Mongolian:  , translit. Buriad Uls) is a federal subject of Russia (a republic), located in Siberia in Asia. Formerly part of the Siberian Federal District, it is now part of the Russian Far East as of November 2018 in accordance with a decree issued by Russian President Vladimir Putin. Its capital is the city of Ulan-Ude. Its area is 351,300 square kilometers (135,600 sq mi) with a population of 972,021 (2010 Census).
