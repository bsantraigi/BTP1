Desnogorsk (Russian: ) is a town in Smolensk Oblast, Russia, located on the right bank of the Desna River 153 kilometers (95 mi) southeast of Smolensk. Population: 29,677(2010 Census); 32,070(2002 Census); 32,302(1989 Census). Desnogorsk is located on the banks of the Desna River and is surrounded by Roslavlsky District.
