Hiram na Alaala (International title: Memories of Love / lit.Borrowed Memory) is a Philippine television drama romance series broadcast by GMA Network. Directed by Dominic Zapata, it stars Dennis Trillo, Kris Bernal, Lauren Young and Rocco Nacino. It premiered on September 22, 2014 on the network's Telebabad line up replacing Ang Dalawang Mrs. Real and on September 22, 2014 (North America) and on September 23, 2014 (Middle East, North Africa, Asia Pacific and Europe) on GMA Pinoy TV. The series concluded on January 9, 2015 with a total of 80 episodes. It was replaced by Second Chances in its timeslot.
