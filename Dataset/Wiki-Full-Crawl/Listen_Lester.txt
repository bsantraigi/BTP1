Listen Lester is a 1924 black-and-white silent film drama/comedy film directed by William A. Seiter, with a screen adaptation by Lewis Milestone and William A. Seiter, based upon the 1918 stage play of the same name.  Released by Universal Pictures on May 20, 1924, the film stars Louise Fazenda and Harry Myers.Prints of the film exist in the Library of Congress film archive.
