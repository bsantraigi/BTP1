Scheila Gonzalez (born August 5, 1971 in Los Angeles, California) is an American, Grammy winning multi-instrumentalist and music educator.  She is best known for playing the saxophone and other instruments with artists such as Dweezil Zappa, Alex Acua, Ray Parker Jr., and many others.  She currently plays alto saxophone in the all-female DIVA Jazz Orchestra and is a full-time member of the Zappa Plays Zappa world tour, in which she sings and plays several instruments.
