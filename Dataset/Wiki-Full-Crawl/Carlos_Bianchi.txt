Carlos Arcecio Bianchi (born 26 April 1949), nicknamed Virrey (Viceroy), is an Argentine former footballer and current manager. A prolific goalscorer, although he had a bright career as a forward in Argentina and France, Bianchi is best known as one of the most successful coaches of all time managing Vlez Sarsfield and Boca Juniors to a great number of titles each. Bianchi is the only coach to win four Copa Libertadores. He most recently served as manager of Boca Juniors.
