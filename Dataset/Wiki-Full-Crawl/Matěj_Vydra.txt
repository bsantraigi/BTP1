Matj Vydra (Czech pronunciation: [macj vdra], born 1 May 1992) is a Czech professional footballer who plays as a striker for English club Burnley and the Czech Republic national team.
He has previously played for FC Chotbo, Vysoina Jihlava, Bank Ostrava, Club Brugge, Udinese and Watford. He has also spent two loan spells at Watford.
