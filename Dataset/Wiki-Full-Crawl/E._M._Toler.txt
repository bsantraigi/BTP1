Enoch McLain Toler, known as E. M. Toler (October 29, 1874  October 21, 1954), was a physician from Clinton, Louisiana, who was from 1944 until his death a decade later a member of the Louisiana State Senate from East and West Feliciana Parish. His tenure corresponded with the administrations of Governors Jimmie Davis, Earl Kemp Long, and Robert F. Kennon.
