Hong Kong Rangers Football Club (Chinese: ), often abbreviated to Rangers, currently known as Biu Chun Rangers due to sponsorship reasons, is a Hong Kong football club which currently competes in the Hong Kong First Division. They have won the Hong Kong First Division once, the Senior Shield 4 times, and the Hong Kong FA Cup twice.
The club plays most of its home matches at Kowloon Tsai Park and Po Kong Village Road Park.
