WMMJ is a Jammin' Oldies leaning Urban Adult Contemporary radio station owned by Radio One in the Washington, D.C., market.  It is co-owned with WKYS, WOL, WPRS-FM, and WYCB and has studios located in Silver Spring, Maryland.  It is licensed to Bethesda, but its transmitter is located in Tenleytown. During the 1960s, '70s, and early '80s, the station had been home to the original "progressive rock" formatted WHFS, which later migrated to the higher powered Annapolis, Maryland-based 99.1 frequency.
WMMJ simulcasts on WDCJ (92.7 FM, Prince Frederick, Maryland) for the southeastern counties of the Washington metro.WMMJ broadcasts in the HD format.
