Wolfgang Loos (born 6 August 1955) is a retired German football player. He spent six seasons in the 2. Bundesliga with Preuen Mnster and VfL Osnabrck.
Since retiring as a player, Loos has worked in various functions for a number of German football clubs, mostly as business manager or executive director, namely for VfL Osnabrck (19861992), Dynamo Dresden (1993), 1. FC Kln (19932002), Eintracht Braunschweig (20022006), 1. FC Saarbrcken (20072008), TuS Koblenz (20082011), and RB Leipzig (20112012). At Eintracht Braunschweig, he also took over as caretaker manager for a short time in 2004.
