Woh Teri Bhabhi Hai Pagle (English: She is your sister-in-law, O Crazy!!!) is an Indian fiction sitcom television series, which premiered on 18 January 2016, and is broadcast on SAB TV. The series is produced by Optimystix Entertainment of Vipul D. Shah. The series airs on every Monday to Friday midnights.Krishna Gokani and Ali Asgar play the female and male lead roles respectively. Krishna Gokani plays the role of a doctor, while Ather Habib becomes a doctor and Ali Asgar becomes a patient with the help of Ashwin Kaushal. The two patient and doctor fall in love with the doctor.
