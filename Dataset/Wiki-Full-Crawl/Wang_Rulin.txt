Wang Rulin (Chinese: ; pinyin: Wng Rln; born April 1953) is a Chinese politician and senior regional official.  He is serving as Vice-Chairperson of the National People's Congress Agriculture and Rural Affairs Committee. From 2014 to 2016, Wang was the Communist Party Secretary of Shanxi province, where he managed anti-corruption efforts as part of the greater anti-corruption campaign under Xi Jinping. Wang formerly served as Party Secretary (201214) and Governor (200912) of Jilin province.
