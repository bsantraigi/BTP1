Gmina Wadysaww is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Turek County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Wadysaww, which lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) north of Turek and 111 km (69 mi) east of the regional capital Pozna.
The gmina covers an area of 90.71 square kilometres (35.0 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 7,807.
