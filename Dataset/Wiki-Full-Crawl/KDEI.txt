KDE () is an international free software community developing Free and Open Source software. As a central development hub, it provides tools and resources that allow to collaboratively work on this kind of software. Well-known products include the Plasma Desktop, KDE Frameworks and a range of cross-platform applications like Krita or digikam designed to run on Unix and Unix-like desktops, Microsoft Windows and Android.
Being one of KDE's most recognized projects, the Plasma Desktop is the default desktop environment on many Linux distributions, such as openSUSE, Manjaro, Mageia, Chakra, Kubuntu, and PCLinuxOS.
