Spas Dzhevizov (Bulgarian:  ; born 27 September 1955, in Plovdiv) is a former Bulgarian footballer who played as a  forward. For the Bulgarian national team he featured in 20 games and scored 3 goals.Between 1976 and 1984 Dzhevizov played in 205 matches and scored 96 goals for CSKA Sofia. Dzhevizov won the top Bulgarian league, the A PFG, four times (all with CSKA), as well as the Bulgarian Cup, two times (all with CSKA). He led the league in scoring during the 197980 season with 23 goals.Following his playing career, Dzhevizov became a manager of clubs in Bulgaria and Cyprus, including CSKA Sofia and Alki Larnaca.
