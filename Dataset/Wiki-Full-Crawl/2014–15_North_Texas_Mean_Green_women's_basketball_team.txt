The 201415 North Texas Mean Green women's basketball team represents the University of North Texas during the 201415 NCAA Division I women's basketball season. The Mean Green, led by third year head coach Mike Petersen, play their home games UNT Coliseum, also known as The Super Pit,  and are second year members of Conference USA. They finished the season 524, 414 in C-USA play to finish in a tie for twelfth place. They failed to qualify for the Conference USA Women's Tournament.
