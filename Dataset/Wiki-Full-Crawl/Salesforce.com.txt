Salesforce.com, Inc. (styled in its logo as salesorce; abbreviated usually as SF or SFDC) is an American cloud-based software company headquartered in San Francisco, California. Though the bulk of its revenue comes from a customer relationship management (CRM) product, Salesforce also sells a complementary suite of enterprise applications focused on customer service, marketing automation, analytics and application development. 
Salesforce was ranked first in Fortune's 100 Best Companies to Work For in 2018.
