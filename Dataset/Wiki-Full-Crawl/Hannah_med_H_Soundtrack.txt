Hannah med H Soundtrack is the soundtrack to the 2003 Swedish drama film Hannah med H, recorded by the Swedish electropop duo The Knife. Made up mostly of instrumentals, the album was released in Sweden on 26 November 2003 by Rabid Records. Some tracks on it also appear on the UK release of the duo's self-titled debut album, as well as on their 2003 album, Deep Cuts, which was being made at the same time.
