Nir Klinger (Hebrew:  ; born 25 May 1966) is an Israeli former footballer, and currently the manager of Beitar Jerusalem.
During the 1990s, Klinger served as Captain for Maccabi Tel Aviv and the Israel national football team.
He was the manager of Maccabi Tel Aviv for three seasons, before resigning under pressure on 5 December 2005. As manager of Maccabi, Klinger won the Israeli championship and an Israeli state cup.
