Pierre Drossi (born 12 October 1959) is a French professional retired football player and current manager.
He played as a defender for Lille OSC, FC Sochaux-Montbliard, OGC Nice, Paris Saint-Germain and AS Cannes.He was head coach of Stade Rennais until he resigned on 16 December 2007, after a 12 defeat against SM Caen.
