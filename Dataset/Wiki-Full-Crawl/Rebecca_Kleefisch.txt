Rebecca Ann Kleefisch (ne Reed; born August 7, 1975) is an American former television news anchor and politician, who is currently serving as the 44th lieutenant governor of Wisconsin. She was elected to the position on November 2, 2010, as the running mate of Governor Scott Walker. She and Walker were the subjects of a recall election on June 5, 2012. Kleefisch is the only lieutenant governor in the history of any state in the United States to face recall election and ultimately survive a recall.
