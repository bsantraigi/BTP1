Jestetten is a town in the district of Waldshut in Baden-Wrttemberg in Germany.
The border crossing into Switzerland is located to the east of town along Schaffhauserstrae leading to Neuhausen am Rheinfall. Another border crossing in the municipality is located south west of town along Osterfingerstrae, towards the villager of Osterfingen in Wilchingen municipality, Schaffhausen canton.
Jestetten railway station is situated on the Swiss Federal Railway's cross-border Eglisau-Neuhausen railway line and is served by Zurich S-Bahn line S22.
