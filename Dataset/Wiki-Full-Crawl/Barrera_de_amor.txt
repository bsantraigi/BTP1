Barrera de amor (English: Barrier of Love) is a Mexican telenovela produced by Ernesto Alonso for Televisa in 2005.On Monday, October 10, 2005, Canal de las Estrellas started broadcasting Barrera de amor weekdays at 8:00pm, replacing Contra viento y marea. The last episode was broadcast on Friday, May 12, 2006 with La fea ms bella replacing it on Monday, May 15, 2006.
Yadhira Carrillo and Sergio Reynoso starred as protagonists, Susana Diazayas and Aarn Daz starred as co-protagonists, while Raquel Olmedo, Alexis Ayala, Gerardo Murgua, Chantal Andere, Armando Araiza and Alexa Damin starred as antagonists. Manuel Landeta and Norma Herrera starred as stellar performances.
