Gmina Jeleniewo, is a rural gmina (administrative district) in Suwaki County, Podlaskie Voivodeship, in north-eastern Poland. Its seat is the village of Jeleniewo, which lies approximately 14 kilometres (9 mi) north of Suwaki and 122 km (76 mi) north of the regional capital Biaystok.
The gmina covers an area of 131.84 square kilometres (50.9 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 3,018.
The gmina contains part of the protected area called Suwaki Landscape Park.
