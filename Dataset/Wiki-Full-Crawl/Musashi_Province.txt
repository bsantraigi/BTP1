Musashi Province (, Musashi no kuni) was a province of Japan, which today comprises Tokyo Metropolis, most of Saitama Prefecture and part of Kanagawa Prefecture. It was sometimes called Bush (). The province encompassed Kawasaki and Yokohama. Musashi bordered on Kai, Kzuke, Sagami, Shimsa, and Shimotsuke Provinces.
Musashi was the largest province in the Kant region.
