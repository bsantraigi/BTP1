Haraldur Freyr Gumundsson (born 14 December 1981) is an Icelandic retired professional footballer who played as a defender. During his career, Gumundsson played in Norway for Aalesunds FK and Start; in Cyprus for Apollon Limassol; and had three separate spells with his 
hometown team Keflavk.He is now manager at Reynir Sandgeri and won the 4 league 2017 with Reynir Sandgeri.
