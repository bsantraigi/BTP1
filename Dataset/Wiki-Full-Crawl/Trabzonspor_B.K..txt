Trabzonspor Basketbol Kulb (English: Trabzonspor Basketball Club), also known as Trabzonspor Basket or Trabzonspor, is a professional basketball club that is based in the city of Trabzon, Turkey. It is a department of the multi-sport club of Trabzonspor. Their home arena is the Hayri Gr Arena, which has a capacity of 7,500 seats, and was opened in 2011.
