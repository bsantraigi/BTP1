Melis Abzalov (Uzbek: Melis Abzalov,  ; Russian:  ; November 18, 1938   October 26, 2016) was an Uzbek actor, film director, screenwriter, and film producer. His most famous films include Suyunchi (1982), Kelinlar qozgoloni (1984), Armon (1986), and Otgan kunlar (1997).
Abzalov is celebrated as one of the founders and prominent members of the Uzbek film making industry. During his lifetime, he received many honorary titles and awards, including the title Meritorious Artist of Uzbekistan (1987).
