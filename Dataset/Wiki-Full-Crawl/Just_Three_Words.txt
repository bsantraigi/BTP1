"Just Three Words" is a song by British-Norwegian A1, released as the first single from the band's fifth studio album, Rediscovered on April 8, 2012. The track is a duet with singer-songwriter Annie Khalid. Although not released to promote the album, and only being available through the iTunes Store as a promotional single, the track was successful across Norway and became the most added track on Norwegian radio on the week beginning April 11, 2012. No music video was released for the single.
