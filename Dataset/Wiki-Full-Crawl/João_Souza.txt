Joo Olavo Soares de Souza (Portuguese pronunciation: [uw so(w)z]; born 27 May 1988) is a Brazilian professional tennis player. Souza competes mainly on the ATP Challenger Tour, both in singles and doubles. He reached his highest ATP singles ranking, No. 69, on April 6, 2015, and his highest ATP doubles ranking, No. 70, on January 7, 2013. Souza is coached by former Brazilian player Ricardo Acioly. Joo Souza is also known as "Feijo" (Portuguese for Bean).
