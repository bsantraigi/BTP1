"Go Getta" is the second single from Def Jam artist Young Jeezy off his second album The Inspiration, it features singer R. Kelly. The song samples "Born On Halloween" by Blue Magic. It was released in late January. This song was #76 on Rolling Stone's list of the 100 Best Songs of 2007.The making of the video (directed by Chris Robinson (director)) was featured on BET's Access Granted on Tuesday February 7, 2007. The Runners, Young Buck, and Slick Pulla made cameos in the video.
