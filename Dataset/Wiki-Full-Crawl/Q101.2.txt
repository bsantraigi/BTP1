Q101.2 FM West  is a radio station based in Market Street, Omagh, County Tyrone  
It broadcasts on the FM frequency on 101.2 to Counties  Tyrone/Fermanagh and the surrounding counties.
Presenters doing live daily shows at the station are Stevie King (7am-11 weekdays) Bryan Gallagher (11am-3pm weekdays) Errol Doherty (3pm-7pm) and Tim Burden 7pm-11pm weekdays  
Q101 is part of the Q radio Network which also owns a number of other stations in Northern Ireland including Q100.5, Q106, Q102.9, Q97.2 and Q107
