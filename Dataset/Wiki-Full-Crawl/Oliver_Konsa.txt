Oliver Konsa (born 4 March 1985 in Tartu)  is an Estonian professional footballer, who last played in Estonian Meistriliiga club JK Nmme Kalju. His contract with his team was suspended, after being arrested in suspicion of drug-related crimes. On 27 September 2013 he pleaded guilty. He was released on 9 January 2014 based on settlement. He played the position of striker and is 1.83 m tall and weighs 74 kg.
