"One Last Score" is the 17th episode in the eighth season, and the 179th overall episode, of the American crime drama television series NCIS. It first aired on CBS in the United States on March 1, 2011. The episode was written by Jesse Stern and directed by Michael Weatherly, and was seen by 19.21 million viewers.In the episode, the team must investigate the death of a former NCIS investigative assistant, while a new NCIS agent is coming back to the Navy Yard.
The episode marks the directing debut for Michael Weatherly, who also portrays Special agent Anthony DiNozzo on NCIS. It also introduces a new female NCIS agent, Special Agent Erica Jane Barret (Sarah Jane Morris), as a recurring character on the show.
