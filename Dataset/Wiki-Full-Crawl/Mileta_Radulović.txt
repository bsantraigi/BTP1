Mileta Radulovi (born 29 January 1981) is a Montenegrin football goalkeeper who is playing for FK Mladost Podgorica in Montenegro.
Born in Titograd, he has played with FK Mladost Podgorica, FK Sutjeska Niki, FK elik Niki, FK Zeta, Serbian side FK BASK, Bosnian FK eljezniar Sarajevo, FK Crvena Stijena, FK Budunost Podgorica, OFK Bar and OFK Grbalj.
