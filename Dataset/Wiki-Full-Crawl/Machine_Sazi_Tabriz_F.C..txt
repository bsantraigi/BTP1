Machine Sazi Tabriz Football Club (Persian:    ) more commonly known as Machine Sazi is an Iranian football club based in Tabriz, East Azerbaijan, Iran, who currently play in the Persian Gulf Pro League. Machine Sazi is the oldest team in the northwest of Iran and is owned by  Rasoul Khatibi.Machine Sazi finished third in the 199495 Azadegan League under the guidance of Iranian legend Nasser Hejazi, their highest ever finish in the Iranian top flight, but were relegated to the second tier after a few years. In 2016, under Rasoul Khatibi, Machine Sazi returned to the Iranian top flight after a 20 year absence.
