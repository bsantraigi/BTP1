Domagoj Kapetanovi was a Yugoslav football player and manager best known for winning the 1976 NASL Championship with Toronto Metros-Croatia.
Kapetanovi started his playing career with Graanski. After the club was disbanded in 1945 he was one of several Graanski players who went on to join the Belgrade-based FK Partizan, along with Franjo Glaser, Zlatko ajkovski, Stjepan Bobek, Miroslav Brozovi and others. He also played for NK Lokomotiva, Dinamo Zagreb and Metalac.
As manager, he coached Dinamo Zagreb in 1973, and several Croat clubs abroad, such as Toronto Metros-Croatia in Canada and Croatia Sydney in Australia.
