Strike Bautista Revilla , born March 2, 1970, in Bacoor, Cavite) is the incumbent Representative of Cavite's 2nd District in the Philippine House of Representatives. He previously served as Mayor of Bacoor.  A member of the  NUP party, he was also a former member of the Board of Directors of the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO) from August 2005 to March 2007.
