Ryuichi Ogata ( , Ogata Ryichi, born December 17, 1985 in Sapporo, Japan) is a Japanese singer, dancer, rapper, and guitarist. In 2001, he rose to fame as a member of the pop group W-inds as the group's dancer, back-up vocalist, and rapper. In 2013, he formed the rock band All City Steppers and is their backing vocalist and guitarist.
