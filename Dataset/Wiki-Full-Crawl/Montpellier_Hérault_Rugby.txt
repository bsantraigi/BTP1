Montpellier Hrault Rugby (French pronunciation: [m.p.lje eo bi klb]) (Occitan: Montpelhir Erau Rugbi Club) is a French professional rugby union club, based in Montpellier, Occitania and named after the Hrault river. The club competes in the top level of the French league system, the Top 14. They originally played at Stade Sabath (capacity 5,000) but moved to the Stade Yves-du-Manoir, later known as Altrad Stadium, and since renamed the GGL Stadium, in 2007. They wear white and blue.
