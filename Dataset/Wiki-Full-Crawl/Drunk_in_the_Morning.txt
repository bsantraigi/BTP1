"Drunk in the Morning" is a single by Danish band Lukas Graham. The song was released in Denmark as a digital download on 13 February 2012. It was released as the second single from their debut self-titled debut studio album. The song peaked to number one on the Danish Singles Chart. The song was written by Lukas Forchhammer, Sebastian Fogh, Stefan Forrest, Morten Ristorp, Magnus Larsson and Mark Falgren.
