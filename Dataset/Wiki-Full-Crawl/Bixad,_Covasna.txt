Bixad (Hungarian: Sepsibkszd, Hungarian pronunciation: [pibyksad]) is a commune in Covasna County, Transylvania, Romania. Composed of a single village, Bixad, it was joined to Malna in 1968. It once again became an independent commune when it split off in 2004. It lies in the Szkely Land, an ethno-cultural region in eastern Transylvania.
