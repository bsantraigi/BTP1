Paul Linger (born 20 December 1974) is an English former professional footballer who played in the Football League as a midfielder for Charlton Athletic, Leyton Orient and Brighton & Hove Albion. He went on to play non-League football for clubs including Welling United, Billericay Town,Braintree Town, Purfleet/Thurrock,East Thurrock United,Wingate and Finchley,Chelmsford City,Redbridge, 
and Dover Athletic.Linger represented the United Kingdom at the 2005 Maccabiah Games.
