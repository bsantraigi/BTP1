Daniel Emmanuel Luduea (born 27 July 1982) also known as El Hachita is a former Argentine-Mexican football attacking midfielder, who last played for Tampico Madero in the Ascenso MX in Mexico.He is the older brother of footballer Gonzalo Luduea and the son of former Talleres de Crdoba player Luis Antonio Luduea.
On July 27, 2013 Luduea made a 70-yard goal against Tigres that proved to be the game winner in a 2-1 win. He also holds Mexican citizenship.On 8 July 2016, Talleres de Crdoba reached an agreement with Pumas for a 6-month-loan of Luduea.
