Maka Kotto (born December 7, 1961), is a politician of Cameroonian descent from Quebec, Canada, who was a Parti Qubcois member of the National Assembly of Quebec for the riding of Bourget.  From 2012 to 2014, he served as the Minister of Culture and Communications.  A former member of the House of Commons of Canada for the Bloc Qubcois, Kotto is also a published author and has appeared in films.
