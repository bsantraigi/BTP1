L'Association Sportive Forezienne Andrzieux-Bouthon is a French association football team founded in 1947. They are based in Andrzieux-Bouthon, Rhne-Alpes, France and are currently playing in the Championnat National 2, the fourth tier in the French football league system. For the 201617 season, they play at the L'Envol Stadium in Andrzieux-Bouthon, which has a capacity of 5000. Previously, they had played at Stade Roger Baudras.
