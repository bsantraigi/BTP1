Celaya Football Club is a Mexican professional football club based in Celaya, Guanajuato. Celaya currently plays in the Ascenso MX. Initially formed on Sunday 7 February 1954, the team was reorganized in Winter 2003 after their city neighbour Atltico Celaya lost its license to Colibres de Morelos and moved to Primera Divisin A, through the moving of La Piedad to Celaya.
