Rubn Daro Larrosa (born 12 April 1979) emerged from the bottom of the Football Club Argentino city Lauquen Dam. Now a professional footballer side currently playing for Italian Football League ASD Aprilia calcium, where he plays as a Forward.
He has played for different clubs throughout the world, such as Juventud de Las Piedras and C.A. Cerro (Uruguay), Gama (Brazil), Hailong (China), UD Marbella (Spain), Persib Bandung (Indonesia), Walsall (England).
