Hasee Toh Phasee (English: She Smiles, She's Snared!; stylised as Hasee  Phasee) is a 2014 Indian romantic comedy-drama film directed by Vinil Mathew and produced by Karan Johar and Anurag Kashyap. The film features Sidharth Malhotra, Parineeti Chopra and Adah Sharma in the lead roles. The film was released on 7 February 2014 and received positive reviews. The movie collected around 620 million (US$8.6 million) worldwide.
