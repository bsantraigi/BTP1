Deiva Vaakku (English: Divine promise) is a 1992 Indian Tamil drama film, directed by M. S. Madhu, starring Karthik Muthuraman and Revathi in lead roles. The film, produced by T. Siva, had musical score by Ilaiyaraaja and was released on 11 September 1992. The film generated average profits at the box office. It is a remake of the Telugu film Sankeerthana.
