Gmina Radziejowice is a rural gmina (administrative district) in yrardw County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland. Its seat is the village of Radziejowice, which lies approximately 10 kilometres (6 mi) south-east of yrardw and 40 km (25 mi) south-west of Warsaw.
The gmina covers an area of 80.06 square kilometres (30.9 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 4,616.
