DZEC Radyo Agila (1062 kHz AM station) is a news/talk radio station in Mega Manila market, which serves as the flagship station of the Radyo Agila Network. It is owned by the Eagle Broadcasting Corporation in the Philippines. The station's studio is located at EBC Bldg., 25 Central Ave., Diliman, Quezon City and its transmitter is located at Barangay Paliwas, Obando, Bulacan. DZEC operates daily (including Summer Break from Thursday to Saturday) from 4am to 12mn.
