Krynica-Zdrj [kritsa zdrui] (until 31 December 2001 Krynica, Rusyn: , Ukrainian: ) is a town in Nowy Scz County, Lesser Poland Voivodeship, Poland. It is inhabited by over eleven thousand people. It is the biggest spa town in Poland often called the Pearl of Polish Spas; and a popular tourist and winter sports destination situated in the heart of the Beskids mountain range.
