Saint milie de Villeneuve (9 March 1811  2 October 1854) was a Roman Catholic French nun and the founder of the Sisters of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception of Castres.
After she died her cause of canonization was opened. She was beatified in 2007 after a miracle attributed to her intercession was ratified and she was beatified in 2009. She was approved for sainthood in 2014 and was canonized by Pope Francis on 17 May 2015.
