Gmina Raszkw is an urban-rural gmina (administrative district) in Ostrw Wielkopolski County, Greater Poland Voivodeship, in west-central Poland. Its seat is the town of Raszkw, which lies approximately 8 kilometres (5 mi) north of Ostrw Wielkopolski and 95 km (59 mi) south-east of the regional capital Pozna.
The gmina covers an area of 134.46 square kilometres (51.9 sq mi), and as of 2006 its total population is 11,275 (out of which the population of Raszkw amounts to 2,037, and the population of the rural part of the gmina is 9,238).
