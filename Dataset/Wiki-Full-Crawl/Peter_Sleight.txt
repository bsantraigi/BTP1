Professor Peter Sleight M.D.(Cantab.), D.M. (Oxon.) FRCP FACC (born 27 June 1929) is a distinguished internationally known research cardiologist  and an Honorary Consultant Physician at the John Radcliffe Hospital in Oxford and the Oxford University Radcliffe NHS Trust. Consultant physician/cardiologist firstly at the Radcliffe Infirmary in Oxford since 1964. Sleight is Emeritus Field Marshal Alexander Professor of Cardiovascular Medicine at the University of Oxford and an Emeritus Fellow of Exeter College, Oxford.
