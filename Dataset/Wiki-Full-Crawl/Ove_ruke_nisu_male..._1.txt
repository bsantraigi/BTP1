Ove ruke nisu male... 1 (These hands are not small... 1) is the first compilation album by the Serbian alternative rock band Disciplina Kime, released by Tom Tom Music in 2000. The compilation features the material released on the first studio album, Svia mi se da ti ne bude prijatno, and the first EP, Ja imam arene oi, as well as live material, recorded at the Zagreb Lapidarij in 1982. The second part of the compilation, Ove ruke nisu male... 2 was released in 2005, by the same record label.
