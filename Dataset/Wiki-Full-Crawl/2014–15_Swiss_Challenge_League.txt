The 2014-15 Brack.ch Challenge League is the 12th season of the Brack.ch Challenge League, the second tier in the Swiss football pyramid. It began on 19 July 2014 and was scheduled to end on 30 May 2015. The league was on winter break between 8 December 2014 and 7 February 2015. Servette were forcibly relegated at the end of the season after failing to acquire a license for the 2015-16 season.
