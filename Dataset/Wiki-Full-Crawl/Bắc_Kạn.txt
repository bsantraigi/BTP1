Bc Kn (listen) is the capital of Bc Kn Province, Vietnam. The province's only city, it is bordered by Bch Thng District to the north, north-east and west and Ch Mi District to the south-east and south-west.The town traces its origins to a fort established in 1880 and upgraded to city on 11 March 2015. It is divided into six wards: Nguyn Th Minh Khai, c Xun, Sng Cu, Phng Ch Kin, Huyn Tng and Xut Ha and 2 communes: Dng Quang, Nng Thng.
