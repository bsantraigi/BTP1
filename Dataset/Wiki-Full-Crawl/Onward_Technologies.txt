Onward Technologies is an Indian Multinational Engineering and IT services company headquartered in Mumbai, INDIA. Onward is a Public Limited Company listed on Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE: 517536) and National Stock Exchange of India Limited (NSE: ONWARDTEC). It is an ISO certified organization from TUV-NORD for ISO-9001:2015 and ISO-27001:2013.
Onward has its delivery centers located at Pune, Chennai & Detroit and Sales offices in Chicago, Boston Detroit, Wisconsin & Ohio in North America, Frankfurt in Germany and Birmingham in UK.
