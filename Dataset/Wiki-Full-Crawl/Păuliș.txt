Puli (Hungarian: plos) is a commune in Arad County, Romania, is situated in the contact zone of the Mure Couloir with the Aradului Plateau and Zrand Mountains. The administrative territory of the commune is 12,806 hectares. The commune centre  is situated at 25 km from Arad. It is composed of four villages: Baraca (Plosbaracka), Cladova (Kalodva), Puli and Smbteni (Szabadhely).
