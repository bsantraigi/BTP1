Amer Sports Oyj (formerly Amer-Yhtym Oyj) is a Finnish headquartered sporting goods company with brands including Salomon Wilson (including subsidiary Louisville Slugger) Atomic Skis Arcteryx Mavic Suunto ENVE Composites Peak Performance and PrecorEstablished in 1950 as an industrial conglomerate with interests as diverse as tobacco trading ship owning and publishing Amer has gradually evolved into a multinational firm devoted to the production and marketing of sporting goods
