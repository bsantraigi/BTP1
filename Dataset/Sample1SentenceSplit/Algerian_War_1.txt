The Algerian War, also known as the Algerian War of Independence or the Algerian Revolution (Arabic:   Al-thawra Al-Jazaa'iriyya; Berber languages: Tagrawla Tadzayrit; French: Guerre d'Algrie or Rvolution algrienne) was a war between France and the Algerian National Liberation Front (French: Front de Libration Nationale  FLN) from 1954 to 1962, which led to Algeria gaining its independence from France.
