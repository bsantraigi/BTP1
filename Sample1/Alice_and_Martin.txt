Alice et Martin  (US title: Alice and Martin) is a 1998 French film, a psychological drama, directed by Andr Tchin. It stars Juliette Binoche and Alexis Loret. It is Tchin's second collaboration with Binoche after the 1985 film Rendez-vous. The plot follows the two title characters, Martin, a male model, and Alice, a struggling violinist. Their romance is shattered when Martin's troubled past begins to haunt him.
