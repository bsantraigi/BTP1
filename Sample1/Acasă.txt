Pro 2 (formerly Acas, meaning "At home") is a television channel operated by Central European Media Enterprises(through WarnerMedia/AT&T), that broadcasts in Romania. It is an entertainment station, primarily dedicated to daytime series, family movies, talk shows, soap operas and news programs. Its sister is the television channel Pro Gold that was launched on 15 April 2012 as Acas Gold; it airs reruns of the telenovelas that aired on Pro 2 and, especially, classical telenovelas.
