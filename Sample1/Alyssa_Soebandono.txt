Alyssa Soebandono (born Anindya Alyssa Soebandono; December 25, 1991), is an Indonesian actress of film-television, presenter and singer who is familiarly called Icha. Soebandono is the third child of four siblings. Her hobbies are playing violin, acting, singing, reading, writing, and swimming. She also has a blog. She has completed her bachelor's degree in Monash University on Australia and now she is planning on taking post-graduate program.
